    Here is an ultimate list of 10 TikTok food accounts to follow. Time to improve those culinary skills!

    • Top 10 Food TikTokers You Must Follow

    @menwiththepot

    Chicken Alfredo with a twiSt 😎 #menwiththepot #fyp #foryou #4u #asmr #foodporn #food #cooking #fire #forest #nature

    ♬ original sound – menwiththepot

    If you like TikTok food accounts, you most probably know these two guys. Slawek Kalkraut and Krzysztof Szymansk are unique type of food TikTokers. They cook their delicious looking food on fire in a forest, using only a few pots and a huge sharp knife. Their videos don’t have typical TikTok songs and effects, only serene natural sounds and taps of knife are heard in their videos. They also don’t talk or show their faces. It is an account full of only food.

    @feelgoodfoodie

    Avocado showing off in this chicken salad! ☺️🥑👌 #HealthyObssession #avocadotoast #chickensalad #lunchideas

    ♬ original sound – Johnny Sibilly

    The name is not “feel good” for nothing. Yumna adopts cooking with healthy and feel-good ingredients as her purpose on this food TikTok account. She explains her approach as:

    “I’m cooking healthier now than I did even a few years back. I feel like we’re all beginning to discover that wholesome, organic, natural, and clean are the secrets to feeding our families well.”

    Definitely one of the food TikTok accounts to follow if you’re into eating healthy!

    @cookingwithshereen

    Iced Lemon Pound Cake #homecafe #hellospring #sugarcrush #easter #dinnerparty #learnontiktok #cookingwithshereen #lemonloaf #foodontiktok

    ♬ original sound – COOKING with SHEREEN

    “Cooking from scratch” is her motto. With detailed recipe videos, Shereen Pavlides makes cooking look  super easy. She has a wide range of recipes from cocktails to snacks and from desserts to salads. It appeals to people of all kind and ages. Thanks to her very energetic attitude and easy-looking recipes, you may feel an urgent sense of cooking after visiting her account.

    @samseats

    Korean fried chicken 🍗 #chicken #food #foodie #cooking #asmr #fyp

    ♬ original sound – Sam Way

    With its sound effects, eye-grabbing video edits and appetizing presentations, this TikTok food account is definitely worth-following. His videos are literal translations of food porn. He mostly cooks food from all around the world. If you are interested in the world cuisine, you should take a look.

    @jameshoffmanncoffee

    How to make the ultimate, refreshing coffee soda. Just don’t let the lid fly across the room…I learnt the hard way #Coffee #CoffeeSoda

    ♬ original sound – James Hoffmann

    If you are interested in the coffee culture, this account is for you. James Hoffmann has been working in coffee industry for more than 15 years now. He is one of the leaders in specialty coffee. On his TikTok account, he gives great tips on brewing coffee, coffee equipment reviews, and different recipes about coffees that you may not see anywhere else.

    @johnrondi

    Reply to @damound32 A SMOKED Four Loko?!? Wait till you see the taste review 😂 💨 @1000oaksbarrelco #johnnydrinks #cocktails #fyp #xyz #fourloko #xy

    ♬ Rip It Up – Little Richard

    JohnRondi is a father-son duo who went viral with their fun TikToks. And honestly, they are definitely pushing the limits of drinks. It’s pretty hard to guess where their uncommon recipes will end up. For those who love drinks and want to try one-of-a-kind recipes, this is the ultimate TikTok account to follow.

    @ballehurns

    it’s like a savory s’more #vegan #glutenfreerecipes #sweetpotatosliders

    ♬ someone – Burbank

    Veganizing your diet is so easy! Halle Burns provides TikTok users a wide range of simple and creative vegan food recipes.

    @myplantbasedkitchen

    Tofu Poke 🥕🍅🥑 #poke #tofupoke #fresh #vegan #plantbased #healthy #hawaiianinspired #WorthTheWait #OneStepCloser #fyp #myplantbasedkitchen #delish

    ♬ Intentions (Instrumental Version) [Originally Performed by Justin Bieber & Quavo] – Elliot Van Coup

    Another vegan food TikTok account we have is myplantbasedkitchen. Refreshing food can be the best choice when the summer sun hits hard. So this is the perfect time to follow this food TikTok account with countless of refreshing plant based recipes.

    @jennymartinezzz

    Puffy Taco! Perfect for 5 de Mayo #texmex #food #fyp #learnontiktok #tacos #puffytacos

    ♬ original sound – Jenny Martinez

    Mexican cuisine is arguably one of the best cuisines in the world. Thanks to Jenny Martinez, it is right in your kitchen now. You can find recipes and tips for all kinds of Mexican food you can think of on this account. And it will definitely whet your appetite.

    @thatdudecancook

    One BLT to rule them all #blt #sandwich #bacon #learnontiktok #cook

    ♬ original sound – Sonny Hurrell

    We can say that Sonny Hurrel offers an interesting kitchen masterclass to TikTok users with his entertaining presentations and narratives. Since he has been in the food industry for a long time, he is a total professional and his TikTok videos are very enjoyable and uplifting.

    This is the end of our best food TikTok accounts list. Who else should be involved in this list? Let us know by commenting below and don’t forget to follow our socials.
