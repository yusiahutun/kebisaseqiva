    You need to find CIX's Mural for the Horizon Wilds Takeover Spring Photo Challenge.

    Forza Horizon 5’s Mexico map is full of painted murals to find. One of them is the eye-catching CIX’s Mural. But where is CIX's Mural in Forza Horizon 5? Let’s find out.

    CIX’s Mural is also the location of the Horizon Wilds Takeover Spring Photo Challenge, so you’ll need to find this location if you want to reap all the rewards.

    Table of Contents

    Our handy guide will help you find CIX's Mural in Forza Horizon 5.

    Where is CIX's Mural in Forza Horizon 5?

    The CIX's Mural can be found in the San Sebastien region south of the map in a dense jungle. If you don’t have unlimited fast travel, the nearest Outpost is the Horizon Wilds Outpost.

    Forza Horizon 5 CIX's Mural location

    CIX's Mural is painted on the side of a building in a village town in San Sebastien. As you exit the jungle, the CIX’s Mural is the first sight you’ll see when you enter the village, so it’s hard to miss this beautiful painting.

    Using the map below, follow the cursor to find the exact location of the CIX’s Mural in San Sebastien:

    To complete the Horizon Wilds Takeover Spring Photo Challenge, you need to photograph any Buggy at CIX's Mural in San Sebastian.

    Any buggy is eligible for this challenge including the Funco Motorsports F9, Alumi Craft Class 10 Race Car, Ariel Nomad, and Penhall The Cholla.

    Your reward for this challenge is the La Cucaracha Car Horn.

    Horizon Wilds Takeover Spring Festival Playlist

    There are some rugged reward cars to win in the Horizon Wilds Takeover Spring Festival Playlist including the Nissan Safari Turbo ’85 (20 points) and Mini X-Raid All4 Racing Countryman ’14 (40 points).

    Other reward cars on offer are the Ford RS200 Evolution ’85, Ford F-150 Hoonitruck Gymkhana 10, Porsche #65 Rothsport Racing 911 "Desert Flyer” #89, Local Motors Rally Fighter ’14, and Hoonigan Chevrolet "Napalm Nova" ’72.

    As well as the Photo Challenge, there's also the Lancer-lot Treasure Hunt to complete.
