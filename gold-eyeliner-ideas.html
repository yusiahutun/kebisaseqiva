    1

    Double lines

    You'd be forgiven for thinking gold liner top and bottom would be OTT, but here's proof it works. Go graphic with your wing and soft along the lash lines for a stunning take on the trend.

    2

    Black and gold

    To make the gold really stand out, simply pair it with black. Rim your lash lines with the metallic shade, alongside black kohl liner applied to the top and bottom waterlines.

    3

    Glitter wing

    For something more experimental, try a gold glitter crease joining a wing. Surprisingly it's much easier to paint than black, as the lines needn't be so sharp.

    Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

    4

    Glitter crease

    However, if wings still feel too tricksy, try your gold glitter creases alone. Partner with black mascara and a nude lip for a chic minimal party make-up look.

    5

    Double flicks

    For something slightly more sophisticated – but still achievable – try splitting your flicks in two. This would work better with a more pigmented gold liner as opposed to a sheer glitter version to achieve definition.

    6

    Sunshine eyes

    If you're loving the current trend for 'sunset eyes', try combining it with some gold liner and a subtle halo of glitter to take on the 'sunshine eyes' movement. Fittingly, it's perfect for after hours.

    Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

    7

    Gold touch

    If you'd rather a subtler take on the trend, simply add some gold to your lower lash lines (before applying your mascara) and the inner corners of the eye for a brightening highlight.
