    21 Aug 2024, 00:00

    Any day of the year is perfect for a fun night of games with your family and friends—no need to save it for special occasions. But what to do if you find yourself away from home and that sky-high pile of the best family board games in your den? Not to worry, there are plenty of classic icebreaker games that require no equipment at all, like a good old-fashioned game of charades. Entertaining for all ages, it can be played at any time and in any place, whether you're on vacation, visiting with your neighbors up the street, or hosting a virtual game night. All you need is a good list of charades ideas (we've got that part covered!).

    First: a quick refresher on the activity for those who need it. In charades, you act out a concept in silence—that’s right, no talking allowed—and the rest of your crew has to guess the chosen word or phrase.

    Before deciding on your charades words or theme, you’ll want to consider the age and skill level of your players. For younger kids, pick easy charades ideas (i.e. everyday activities, musical instruments) they’ll be likely to guess. Charades ideas for adults, on the other hand, can include more abstract ideas, such as movie titles and favorite songs. (You’ll also always score proverbial points with your crew for acting out something hilarious.)

    If you’re ready to take charades to a whole new level, we’ve got just the thing to kickstart the experience: over 170 creative and funny charades ideas for kids and adults.
