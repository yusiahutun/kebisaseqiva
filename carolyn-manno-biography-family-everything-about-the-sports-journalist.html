    Carolyn Manno is a name that inspires many budding sports reporters. She is iconic and phenomenal when it comes to sports reporting and has carved out a niche for herself in a male-dominated field. Carolyn Manno is currently covering a series of reports for the NBC Group. During her career, she has covered major events such as the NFL, the Olympic Games (2012, 2016), the Sochi Games 2014, the NBC Super Bowl, the NHL, and the Stanley Cup. Here you will find everything you need to know about the sports journalist Carolyn Manno.

    Carolyn Manno – Biography

    The beautiful and phenomenal Carolyn, whom we have come to love, was born in Florida on July 16, 1984. She remembers that she grew up with great interest and a love for sports. She also admits that she was a little “boyish” during her childhood. Manno was very active and even played soccer when she was in high school. After graduating high school, Carolyn went to the prestigious University of Florida to study telecommunications. She graduated in 2006.

    Carolyn’s love of sports was her main motivation. She knew that she wanted to do sports. When she wasn’t playing actively, reporting was the next best thing. She started her career in journalism in South Bend Indiana. The pretty budding reporter covered a few major sports teams such as the Chicago Bears, Cubs, Notre Dame Fighting Irish, White Sox, to name a few.

    She also had the opportunity to improve her limited knowledge and experience, as her coverage was not limited to a specific sport. She worked at South Bend Indiana for a total of three years before moving on to greater opportunities. Carolyn Manno found her way to the Comcast Sports Network. There she worked as a reporter and sports news anchor. Carolyn Manno did not let herself be intimidated by any sports-related task and went into her new role with verve, which she mastered with flying colors. She covered the Super Bowl, the NBA finals, the Boston Bruins, the Stanley Cup, and the Boston Celtics, among others. She also began writing for CSNNE.com as a writer.

    The spirited sports reporter soon came back into a better position. She joined NBC Sports Group in August 2009. This was a smart career move for Carolyn Manno as it helped take her profession to a new level. She initially worked as a presenter and correspondent before joining NBC and NBCSN full-time as a presenter and reporter in 2013.

    Carolyn Manno has a wide range of responsibilities at NBC Sports Group. She covers NBC’s Sports Update Desk, NASCAR America, Football Night in America, Triple Crown Horse Racing, Super Bowl XLIX, college football, coverage of the Dew Tour, and cycling, among others.

    Carolyn has demonstrated skill and dedication in her work, which has been recognized and rewarded over time. She has won the Indiana Spectrum Award for Journalistic Excellence from the Indiana Broadcasters Association and also the Edward R. Murrow Award for Sports Journalism.

    Family

    Although she is active in social media, Carolyn has refrained from sharing personal information about herself. Apart from memories of her childhood, which she shared with others through occasional interviews, details about her family have never been made public.

    Facts

    • Olympic Stints

    Carolyn Manno is one of the few female reporters who covered the Olympic Games back-to-back. Her first Olympics were in London in 2012 and then in Rio in 2016.

    • Relationship Status

    Being a sports journalist has its advantages and disadvantages. It involves a lot of traveling to cover different games in different places. This could be one of the reasons why Carolyn is not in a steady relationship. It is also possible that she simply chose not to share this aspect of her life with the public. What is certain, however, is that she is not married.

    Carolyn Manno is very fit, which she has achieved thanks to a strict exercise or dietary routine. She is 5 feet and 6 inches tall and is admired for her long, taut legs.

    Carolyn Manno has a big heart. She has dedicated her life to various causes that are important to her. Manno is a member and supporter of NGOs such as Orphan Motivational and Blood for All. She sometimes supports them with her personal funds.
