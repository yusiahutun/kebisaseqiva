    Luca Dotti is a well-known celebrity kid. He is the son of the late British actress and humanitarian Audrey Hepburn and Italian psychiatrist Professor Andrea Dotti. Luca’s mother, Audrey, was a movie star and a renowned fashion icon.

    The American Film Institute ranked her as the third-greatest female screen legend from the Classical Hollywood Cinema, and she was inducted into the International Best Dressed Hall of Fame.

    While Luca Dotti has gained fame as a renowned graphic designer, he is most famous as the son of the iconic British actress. In addition, he is a controversial guy who once labeled his mother as a “Battle-hardened badass.” Since he decided to pursue a career in the entertainment industry like his mother, he has gained the attention of fans and media alike.

    Here’s everything we know about him.

    Who Is Audrey Hepburn’s son?

    Luca Dotti was born in Lausanne, Switzerland, on February 8, 1970. Hence, he is 53 years old. He is the son of the legendary actress Audrey Hepburn and Andrea Dotti. Luca grew up alongside his brother, Sean Hepburn Ferrer, and his parents in Lausanne. Although his mom was an accomplished actress, he grew up in a close-knit family with both parents present in his life. 

    Despite being a celebrity in his own right, Audrey Hepburn’s son has not revealed any details regarding his educational background. Consequently, there are no details regarding his academic achievements. However, online reports suggest he is well-educated. While further information regarding his upbringing remains unknown, Luca’s close-knit family bonds have undoubtedly shaped his journey.

    What Does Luca Dotti Do for a Living?

    Luca Dotti decided to pursue a career in the entertainment industry like his mother. He is a renowned graphic designer and no stranger to the limelight. However, his fame isn’t solely due to his professional accomplishments, as he is famous as the son of the legendary “My Fair Lady” star.

    With an impressive portfolio of design projects, Luca has left his mark on various ventures, including creating the Audrey Hepburn Children’s Fund emblem. In addition, he utilized his artistic skills to curate a captivating cover for his literary masterpiece “Audrey at Home: Memories of My Mother’s Kitchen.”

    Aside from being a gifted designer, Luca Dotti also possesses a remarkable talent for storytelling as an author. His latest work, “Audrey at Home: Memories of My Mother’s Kitchen,” graced bookshelves in 2021.

    This heartfelt tribute serves as a window into the life of his beloved mother and encompasses treasured recipes from her culinary sanctuary. Luca’s brother, Sean, contributed a special foreword to this exceptional creation to further amplify its sentimental value. 

    In addition, Luca is a social worker and a devoted humanitarian. He serves as the Chair of the Audrey Hepburn’s Children’s Fund, a charitable organization established by the family in honor of Audrey a year after her passing.

    Beyond Audrey Hepburn’s renowned style and successful Hollywood career, her philanthropic spirit continues to thrive through the dedicated efforts of her son, Luca Dotti. The late Hollywood diva’s commitment to humanitarian work was a profound aspect of her life that kept her passionately engaged until her final days.

    Who Is Luca Dotti’s Brother?

    Luca’s brother, Sean Hepburn Ferrer, is an American film producer and author. He was born on June 17, 1960, and is the son of Audrey Hepburn and Mel Ferrer. Before marrying Luca’s dad, Professor Andrea Dotti, Audrey Hepburn was previously married to Mel Ferrer. 

    Like his mother, Sean pursued a career in the entertainment industry. However, he mainly works behind the scenes. He has worked over the years developing, producing, and marketing several films such as “Racehoss,” “Growing Pains,” and “Eye of the Widow.”

    Like Luca Dotti, he also published a children’s book in 2020 titled “Little Audrey’s Daydream.” Sean is a proud father and husband. He is currently married to Karin Ferrer, who is his third wife.

    They have three children: Audrey Hepburn’s grandchildren, a girl named Emma, and two sons, Santiago and Gregorio Ferrer. In addition, Sean and his half-brother Luca founded the Audrey Hepburn Children’s Fund after their mother died.

    Is Luca Dotti Married?

    Yes, he is. Audrey Hepburn’s second son, Luca Dotti, married his sweetheart Domitilla Bertusi, and they live in Rome. Luca’s wife, Domitilla Dotti, hails from a prestigious family in Florence, Italy. The couple had their wedding in Argentario, Tuscany. However, there is no detailed information about the exact date of their marriage.
