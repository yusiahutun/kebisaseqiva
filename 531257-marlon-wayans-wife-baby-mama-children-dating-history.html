    Who is Marlon Wayans’ wife?

    Marlon Wayans doesn’t have a wife, and he has never been married. This comes as a surprise to many people who assumed that he was married to his long-time girlfriend, Angela Zachary.

    In an interview with Essence in 2021, the actor stated that he never married because he knew his mother needed him.

    On her death bed, I said, ‘Can I tell you something, woman?’ She was in much pain, and she was fighting to stay alive, and I say, ‘I’m 47 years old, and I’ve never been married because I always wanted you to be my number one girl. My mother turned over, looked at me, grabbed my hand, and pulled me close to her. I said, 'I know, I love you too. I kissed her and said, 'I'll see you soon, but not that soon because I got a s--- load of life to live.' She laughed in pain, and I kissed her, and those were my last words to my mom.
