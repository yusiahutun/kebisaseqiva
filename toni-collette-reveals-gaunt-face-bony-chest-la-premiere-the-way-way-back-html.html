    Disappearing act: Toni Collette reveals gaunt face and bony chest at the LA premiere of The Way, Way Back

    She is known for healthy figure and attainable good looks.

    But Toni Collette cut a worryingly gaunt figure on Sunday night.

    The 40-year-old looked very drawn in the face and revealed a bony chest at the Los Angeles premiere of her new film The Way, Way Back, a far cry from her usually curvy frame.

    Under pressure: Toni Collette looked noticeably gaunt at the LA premiere of The Way, Way Back on Sunday, in contrast to her usually healthy curves, pictured in 2010 in LA

    The Muriel's Wedding star wore an all-black ensemble consisting of baggy trousers and a long wrap top.

    She paired the outfit with black heels, a structured black clutch and silver bracelet.

    The actress wore her strawberry blonde hair wavy over her shoulders and opted for black eyeliner and a nude lip.

    A bit thin: The 40-year-old looked drawn in the face as she posed on the red carpet at the event

    Effortless style: Toni wore loose-fitting black trousers and a long wrap top paired with heels and a clutch

    Beaming: Toni appeared to be in good spirits as she waved and smiled for the crowd

    But it was the star's gaunt face and bony chest that drew attention, a marked change from her usually healthy, fuller appearance.

    Perhaps it was due to her hectic schedule as the mother-of-two jets between LA and Australia, where she is busy filming new miniseries Devil's Playground.

    Her new film The Way, Way Back, which also stars Sam Rockwell and Amanda Peet, is about a teenager who befriends a water park manager while on a summer holiday.

    She arrived in the US earlier this month to start filming for new projects Defiant and Miss You Already, as well as new CBS drama Hostages.

    Good friends: Toni looked thrilled to run into her costar Allison Janney at the premiere

    Having a laugh: The pair giggled as they sat together at a table
