    May 1 is May Day, Lei Day in Hawaii, and it's Tim McGraw's birthday! The country superstar shot to fame when "Indian Outlaw" hit the earbuds of country fans, and his music has been a constant in the genre ever since.

    He married fellow country singer Faith Hill in 1996, and their Soul2Soul II Tour became the highest-grossing tour in country music history, cementing the couple as country music royalty. McGraw has also found success on the big screen, acting in films such as the Oscar nominated film The Blind Side, Country Strong and others. But his biggest accomplishment of all?
