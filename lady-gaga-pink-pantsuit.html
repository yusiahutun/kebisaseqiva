    Lady Gaga channeled her inner sultry Barbie today when she stepped out in Barcelona wearing a sexy pink pantsuit by Versace. The look was from the brand's Spring 2018 tribute collection to Gianni Versace, which commemorated and revived some of his most iconic '90s designs. Mother Monster paired the suit with a thick gold chain necklace by the brand, platform sandals, a black leather tote bag, and aviator sunglasses.

    The best part? This was just an airport look for Gaga. The singer was spotted working the monochrome ensemble after touching down in Spain. So while us average travelers stick to our comfy leggings and cozy layers on the plane, Gaga is out here serving runway looks at an altitude of 39,000 feet.

    BACKGRID

    Though Gaga is the last person to wear the same outfit twice, she has channeled similar rosy looks in the past. There was this iconic glittery ensemble from March 2014, complete with a wide-brim hat and wayfarer sunglasses for blocking out the haters, an ultra-long ponytail, and platform boots that could crush your soul in one step. Sans her shades and shoes, every item in her Christian Cowan-Sanluis-designed look was done in Barbie-hued sparkles. Was the outfit extra? Sure. But was Gaga committed? Absolutely.

    Getty Images

    Then there was this rocker iteration in June 2015, which featured a hushed pink studded blazer and pants layered over a ripped graphic tee and ladylike gloves.

    Splash News

    And last but not least, she showed how the look can be versatile with a pair of shorts. In October 2016, she stepped out in this millennial pink cowgirl-meets-corporate Barbie lewk at the peak of her Joanne era.

    Getty Images

    If this pink look is just the beginning of Gaga's stunning 2018 style, we can't wait to see what she wears next.
