    There are many things about Africa that stand out, but the rich cultural heritage, fashion, and natural resources always top the list. Well, indeed, Africa has had one of its major selling points being the African wear style. The world has been shaken by the eye-catching styles from the fashion industry. If there is anything any African will wear with pride, it is the African wear designs. Here are some of the latest African wear designs to draw inspiration from.

    Not only is the African print style unique but it is also very diversified. There are many print styles to choose from and African fabric ranges from Kente, Gele, Dashiki, Ankara, Kitenge, Khanga among many others. The fabrics were originally handwoven but technology has come to the rescue as manufacturing industries do it effortlessly, furthermore, bulk production is now possible. Fashion trends apparently point to the undeniably colorful and attractive variety of traditional African wear designs for men, children or women which has made the clothing for men, women, and children to die for. One of the notable designs of all time is the African couple wear.

    The African couple wear collection, just like any other wear, has had a wide spectrum of styles. Men and women choose to rock clothes of a similar fabric and even go all the way to making a matching attire for their kids, should they be in the picture. It is beautiful to see the shared love and affection be publicly and tactfully displayed in their African print attires. Below is the African fashion wear for couple all around Africa, incorporating the Ghanaian designs, Nigerian styles, and all the way to the Xhosa fashion.

    Cool African couples wear ideas that rock

    People bonded in love and blood tend to share a few things here and there. There is a school of thought that seeks to establish that the more a man and a woman live together, the more they get to resemble each other. This theory, to some extent, holds some water as it clearly evident among couples who have been together for more than two decades. The basis of this 'theory' is because of sharing things together; same food, house, pain, happiness, love, and what have you. This is no wonder you are likely to observe some tendencies of wearing similar outfits among couples – African couple wear.

    Usually, the African wear styles for couples are eye-catching. The outstanding designs will obviously leave many glaring at you enviously. Besides, the African fashion wear fabrics are rich in cementing the element of undisputed beauty, and despite the style that is chosen, the outfit is picturesque and grand in aesthetics. Below are some of the African wear designs for guys and ladies that are up to the minute, popular and widespread;

    1. Dark matched Ankara African wear styles for couples

    The whole idea in the African couple wear is to put on something of the same fabric on each one's outfit, although each individual goes for their own style. The Ankara fabric has a whole range of African prints in animal, patterned or floral form from where one can choose their preference. Having settled on a particular fabric, it is then easy to make a cloth. Ideally, the woman can choose various African wear dress designs ranging from the mermaid tube dresses, cape dresses to fancy dresses. The man, on the other hand, can choose a casual, semi-formal or formal look. Remember the element of the Ankara fabric must be present in the two outfits. There are those who may choose to go bold on African fabric alone but that does not mean incorporating a matching monotone color fabric will do any harm.

    2. Dashiki shirt with matching pattern dress for the lady

    If you want to look great in a simple way, you can try this chic and easy to pull off men African wear with a matching dress for the lady. The African wear designs for guys in this look ensures the men keep their favorite khaki or jeans pants and top it up with a Dashiki shirt top, while the lady complements the look with a flirty knee-length dashiki dress. More harmony is attained by the different suede shoes; loafers for the man and trendy high heels for the lady.

    3. Short sleeved Dashiki kimono for her and matching Dashiki shirt for him

    Dashiki is one of the modern African wear print fabric that is never boring. A Dashiki fabric, essentially, can be of any color, but the notable element is that there is a central print from which most clothes are cut in such a way that the print will be visible to bring out its beauty. In recent times, this style has been trendy. The woman can have a Dashiki made dress, say of the high low designs. Alternatively, she can have other designs: bodycon, long ball, short skater, among many others The men in this context wear a shirt or a Dashiki coat to complement the lady. Pants can be monotones and the look accessorized as per their desires.

    4. Embroidered African fashion wear tops with round neck

    This is probably one of the native wear for couples of all time, but one you can never go wrong with. Ideally, the African fabric of choice, in this case, should be of a single color. Mark you, there are many colors one can choose from, hence no limitation. The fabric is then embroidered with a striking color thread in simple or otherwise pattern around the chest and arms and in some cases, the African wear for women can have the embroidery done at the hem of the dress. The general idea is that the outfits match in color and the embroidery designs work together. The man ordinarily has the round-collared to high-neck casual shirt while the woman has either the top or the dress embroidered. If, say, the lady wants to match exactly with the man, she can have a top and pants while the man has the shirt and pants. Alternatively, the dress will also do a lot of service, if dresses are your go-to wear.

    5. Hooded sweatshirts with African print patch

    There is nothing cool in the day than rocking that cool hood. It is great in cold wear and stylish too. Anybody who regards themselves as fashionable should at least have it in their wardrobe. But what of the matching hooded sweatshirts for couples? Well, am sure you have seen this style around, admired it and you are probably thinking of getting one for yourself and yours.

    6. Matching sweatshirts with African print on the front

    The hoods are given an African touch by having an African fabric knitted into it. In most cases, either the pockets, the arms, and the hood or the collar area can have the African fabric. The African print is done skillfully to give it a meaningful and purposeful African taste.

    7. Patched African wear hoodies for couples, full African fabric pocket

    Patchwork has come of age in the 21st century and designers have taken their skills and resources into getting more creative about the patchwork on clothes. This is a modern African wear that has taken root within a very short time.

    8. Normal fabric dress, shirt and trouser with African print patches

    Patchwork in the African couple wear simply means that a whole made outfit has some other African print fabric cut accordingly and stitched into it. For instance, if the couple loves butterflies, then a butterfly cut fabric can be patched on their outfits. However, most of the patchwork is done in love symbols or maps of countries or continents, although this does not mean that the designs dictate that which can be patched into your cloth. Some couples even have their pet names or each other's names patched at the back or even at the front of their outfits. This is clearly a show-stopping thing in the African fashion wear. It is trendy and I have observed a popularity for this style among couples, it’s probably time you tried this out.

    9. Denim with subtle African print strips

    Sometimes designers take their creativity and innovation a notch higher and the populace is appreciative of their efforts. We all know that the denim is trendy in this day and age. Everybody is out looking for a denim wear be it in pants, shirts or blouses; the style is grand. From the young to the old, this style is awesome and it is interesting that denim is a style of the olden days that has recently been in the fashion scene; truly, old is gold. In the African couple wear collection, clothing stores have various denim and African-blended outfits that a couple can wear for casual dates, picnics etc. The African print is factored into the wear and two pieces are made from the same fabric. One is for the man and the other is for the lady. Commonly, the front pocket, arm edges, collar, shoulder length, and the buttonhole areas are where the African print is majorly stitched.

In fact, the accessories of the same African fabric can be used, let's say, the head wrap, wristband, handbag, belt, watch, and so on. This style is common among young couples who are more cautious about how they look and do not mind going an extra mile to bring sparkle into their grooming habits.

    10. Kaba African wears styles for couples

    Kaba styles are a West African signature style for couples. Well, we all know that West Africa is rich in the fashion heritage and has exported their fashion sense all over the world, with a warm reception into it. Well, there are some ethnic styles of how men and women used to wear and primarily if the two were a couple. The Kaba styles are simply African print made outfits for couples. Ordinarily, the fabrics used are monochromatic with two to three shades of the same color. In the African wear for women, the fabric is made into a cloth, a dress to be precise. The dress is long, up to the ankle level, straight, or mermaid with a wrap-up. The key thing with this ladies African wear design is that it enhances their feminine looks. Men, on the other hand, have outfits made of similar fabric which usually consists of a casual shirt, and in most cases, with matching pants. This African wear is common for weddings, customary events, and even religious events. However, you can rock it for any other occasion that you deem fit. The emergent trend that is surprising is that this style was conventionally reserved for the elderly people but the youth have shown an impressive interest in the style.

    11. Maxi dress and a matching color pants and shirt with African print patchwork

    The woman has a diverse way in which her modern African wear for couples look can be. The trendy and stylish design for the African wear dresses includes the long maxi dresses with an off shoulder design and puffed up sleeve, bodycon sleeveless, to long sleeves designs with a deep v neck, short flared dresses, fancy dresses, and floor-length gowns, just to mention but a few designs. She can complete the look with minimum accessories and makeup. The modern African wear for ladies is ideal for celebratory activities, religious, and casual occasions. On the other hand, the male species with the African print fashion has a wide range of outfits to rock. To complement the woman, the man can wear the classic suit with elements of African print, for instance, a lapel, tie, waistband, bag, or even the African print hats. He can also wear and African print shirt and pants that match up with the woman. This still looks good and anybody will still tell from afar who is who’s.

    12. Native traditional African wear for couples

    And back we are to our roots, our pride our heritage –the native traditional African wear styles for couples. In as much as the world is getting overly Westernized, modernized, or is it civilized, we all have a social community we identify with. For the preservation of the heritage, native clothing, for instance, is held with a lot of significance. The traditional African wear for couple takes us back to the old days when sewing machines were not there. The cloth would be hand woven and wrapped around the body. The cloth for both the man and woman would be of the same fabric so that it cordially speaks of their unity in love.

    13. Wrap around African fabric with lace and matching dress with lacey top

    The woman, for instance, can put on a sleeveless dress then wrap the fabric around her body and still achieve the native look in a modern way, and the man can similarly do the same. Although a native look would mean that the look is completely ethnic based, there are some off-beat ways to wear this African couple wear. The hair may be African or one may have the contemporary hairdos. The shoes, again, need not be African, although if you deem it fit, you are then good to go. For the man, the authority walking stick and hat would speak volumes if their desire is to show the world the leadership prowess in him. This style is not only loved for being lined back to our cultural heritage but also for its uniqueness and aesthetics. This can be a great African wear for wedding.

    14. Casual and sexy fancy African couple wear

    Not every African couple wear is for the very formal events, some you just rock them for your own pleasure. For instance, if you are attending a party as a couple, you obviously would dress more playfully than you would have in a religious ceremony. In such cases, you need to check closely the occasion and the ability to accommodate crazy Africa couple wear ideas. Just as the name suggests, the African fashion wear, in this case, is flirty and sexy. The woman can rock an African print high-low top, or laced mermaid African print dresses, long maxi off shoulder dresses with a high cut, high low designs or the short flared dresses. However, the dress is not the only thing a woman can wear, she can also wear top and pants in African print, a playful jumpsuit, fancy African print top styles, among other things.

    15. Clear white with simple print family African fashion wear

    In as much as a couple need to look alike, if there are children in the picture, then they need not feel out of place, or rather, lose their sense of belonging. This should be so, especially if a couple is attending a function as a family. In fact, it would appear weird for the kids to wear differently from their parents, unless, of course, they all go for different styles. The family modern African wear does not restrict the family to a certain design or style, although they may choose to slay in the same vein. However, if the parents have different tastes with that of their kids, they may then each go for their designs of preference, so long as the fabric used to make the outfits is similar. For instance, the woman can go for a long maxi dress and the daughter in the picture can wear an African skater dress, all of the same print. The man and, say, a son in the family can wear round neck body fitting shirts with pants of a matching color. This would be a perfect image of African wear.

    16. The all-powerful senator African wear look

    This an African wear for men who are cool and poised. A single fabric is used to make the outfit – a shirt and pants, there cannot be much artwork on it, but it is made in such a way that it is cozy. African print accessories such as a hat or scarf can be used to make it an African couple wear design. The subtle look means that you can use this look as an African dinner wear for men.

    If there is something African traditions are best known for, it is family. Nothing cements this notion better than African wear for couples. Knowing what to wear at the right time will not only make you the glamorous couple but also give you a better sense of belonging and a solid fashion grounding.
