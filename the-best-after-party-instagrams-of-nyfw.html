    New York Fashion Week is officially in full swing, which means a non-stop flurry of presentations, appointments, and of course, runway shows for the fashion set. Not all the action this week circles around the Spring/Summer ’16 collections, however — a quick survey of our Instagram feeds revealed that while the fashion industry is working hard this week, they’re playing hard as well. Rihanna threw a NYFW block party with her new squeeze, Travis Scott, while Marc Jacobs‘ Studio 54-themed party was the talk of the town, with plenty of glitter and debauchery to spare. While there’s still lots of festivities to be had (NYFW has only just begun, after all,) here are the buzziest fetes of the week, so far.
