    Olivier Awards 2024: Full winners list revealed

    And the results are in!Uniting the biggest and brightest stars in theatre, the 2024 Olivier Awards were held on Sunday 14 April with Cara Delevingne, James Norton, David Tennant and more walking the red carpet at the Royal Albert Hall.

    12 May 2024, 00:00

    Sunset Boulevard, Guys & Dolls and Operation Mincemeat went head to head on Sunday night

      And the results are in! Uniting the biggest and brightest stars in theatre, the 2024 Olivier Awards were held on Sunday 14 April with Cara Delevingne, James Norton, David Tennant and more walking the red carpet at the Royal Albert Hall.
