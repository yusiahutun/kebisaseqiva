    KUCCPS students portal: registration, course revision, and charges

    The KUCCPS students portal offers different services to students. These include revision of courses, application, and registration, the student login, KUCCPS application, results, admission list and inquiry, and inter-university transfer.

    Kenya Universities and Colleges Central Placement Service (KUCCPS) is the body responsible for placing Kenyan students in public universities and TVET colleges once the KCSE results are out. To make it easy for students to access the information or make course applications, they must have a student portal.

    What does KUCCPS entail?

    KUCCPS is an entity established in 2012 under the University Act to coordinate government-sponsored students' placement into various undergraduate, diploma, and certificate programmes offered in Kenyan institutions. It was established after dissolving the Joint Admission Board (JAB).

    JAB was the leading player in placing students in universities before KUCCPS was formulated. The institution does its best to serve every student by availing of all the requirements on its website. KUCCPS also provides inter-institution transfer for students who wish to transfer to other institutions of higher learning.

    KUCCPS student portal registration

    To use the KUCCPS portal, you must first do KUCCPS portal registration to be logged in. Below are the steps on how to register on the KUCCPS student portal.

  • Click on the topmost part of the website labelled login.
  • Next, type in your KCSE number and your exam year.
  • Lastly, type your password.

    • It is worth noting that your initial password should be your birth certificate number or your KCPE index number in case you don't have your birth certificate number.

    How to login to KUCCPS

    Once you are through with KUCCPS account creation or registration, you can log in to their portal using the following steps.

  • Go to KUCCPS official website.
  • Click on KUCCPS student portal on the top right corner of the Home Page.
  • Click on the LOGIN tab on the top right corner of the login page.
  • Login by entering your index number, year of registration, and password.

    Once you log in, you can apply for KUCCPS admission to the institution and course programme of choice, check your admission or placement status, and download the student application guide.

    Additionally, you will be able to download your admission letter and check courses offered at the institutions, among other things.

    How much should you pay for KUCCPS?

    You need to part with some money when applying for the first time or when applying for a revision. KUCCPS charges Ksh. 1500for the first applicants and Ksh. 1000 for revisions. You can use the below steps to make the payment.

    • First, go to the M-PESA menu on your mobile phone.
    • Select the Pay Bill option.
    • Next, key in the Business no., which is 820201.
    • Enter your four-digit exam year for the account number, followed by the 11 digits index number.
    • Put in the amount. (Kshs. 15000 for first-time applicants or Kshs. 1000 for revisions).
    • Lastly, key in your M-PESA PIN.
    • Complete by sending.

    Does KUCCPS offer loans to students?

    No, KUCCPS only places students in public universities or colleges. They don't offer loans to students.

    What is the difference between HELB and KUCCPS?

    KUCCPS is a body that places students for admission to institutions of higher learning, while HELB is a body that offers funding options to the admitted students.

    How many courses should I choose in KUCCPS?

    Students with an overall grade of C+ and above can make a maximum of 8 choices of both degree and diploma programmes. Those with an overall C- and above grade but below C+ can make a maximum of 6 choices from diploma programmes.

    What happens after applying in KUCCPS?

    After an application, successful candidates will be notified via text messages and the KUCCPS student portal. All they need to do is to login into their portal or send their index number to 20842 starting with their year of KCSE.
