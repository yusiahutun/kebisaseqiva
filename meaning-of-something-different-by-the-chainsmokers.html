    Meaning of Something Different by The Chainsmokers

    27 Jun 2024, 00:00

    The Chainsmokers' song "Something Different" explores the theme of finding someone who stands out from the rest and brings a unique energy into your life. It is a celebration of the special qualities that make someone different and the impact they can have on your world.In the first verse, the lyrics express admiration for someone who is unafraid to speak their mind and dance like nobody is watching. This sets the tone for the song, highlighting the theme of individuality and authenticity. The lyric "Someone like you, so hard to find, I can't believe I even found you" emphasizes the rarity of discovering someone who possesses these qualities. It suggests that finding someone who is unapologetically themselves is a remarkable and fortunate occurrence.The pre-chorus lyrics "I'm trying to do this every FaceTime, you know that you had me from the first line" convey the act of constantly wanting to connect with the person because their presence is so captivating. This lyric develops the theme of connection and the desire to maintain it. Despite the medium being FaceTime, it symbolizes the need to constantly engage with the person, highlighting the depth of their impact.Moving into the chorus, the repetition of the line "There's something different 'bout you" emphasizes the uniqueness of the person being celebrated in the song. It implies that this person possesses qualities and characteristics that set them apart from others. The lyric "Need your voice on repeat, all that good energy, all of the aura you bring" showcases the positive energy and influence they have on the narrator's life. It suggests that their voice and presence bring joy and inspiration, establishing a theme of positivity and upliftment in the song.In the second verse, the lyrics "You're never one to criticize, even if somebody doubts you" highlight the individual's unwavering support and encouragement. This emphasizes the theme of acceptance and the power of a non-judgmental presence. The line "You watch 'The Crown' like it's about you" adds a playful element to the song, showcasing the person's ability to fully immerse themselves in experiences and make them their own.The bridge lyrics "I try to do this every night, when I call you 'cause I need to hear your voice, I was running 'til I fell into your void" convey a sense of dependency and reliance. It suggests that the person has become an essential part of the narrator's life, filling a void they may have previously felt. This further develops the theme of the transformative power of a unique connection.Overall, "Something Different" celebrates the beauty of finding someone who stands out from the crowd, embraces their individuality, and brings a special energy into your life. The song explores themes of authenticity, connection, acceptance, and the transformative power of a unique bond. It encourages listeners to cherish and celebrate those who bring a different flavor to their world, reminding us of the profound impact that such individuals can have.
