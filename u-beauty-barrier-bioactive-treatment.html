    In a world where skincare remains ever-evolving, it’s important to reach for a brand where innovation and science are at the forefront. Products like these consistently pave the way for revolutionary products that deliver healthy, youthful, glowing skin. Our favorite innovator has got to be U Beauty, a leader in creating skincare that truly transforms the skin using proprietary technology, a sustainable promise, and a need to simply one’s own regimen.

    One product taking the beauty world by storm is U Beauty’s Barrier Bioactive Treatment. The treatment has garnered quite the buzz from reviewers who have seen absolutely incredible results. But to understand why the Bioactive Treatment is such a game-changer, we have to take a look at its ingredients.

    The formula features the brand’s SIREN Capsule Technology, a proprietary blend of functional, bioactive marine ingredients that are engineered to work with the skin’s stem cells and essential growth factors to boost the proliferation of new cells, per the brand. Basically, U Beauty’s Barrier Bioactive Treatment first targets any damaged areas of the skin to remove them, leaving the healthy part of the skin barrier alone. Simultaneously, messenger molecules journey to the skin’s own stem cells to renew and improve skin. Other notable ingredients in this formula include urea, vitamin B6, shea butter, hyaluronic acid, jojoba wax, sunflower wax, and mimosa wax, which collectively help to nourish and hydrate the complexion.

    Ingredients aside, the brand’s own clinical trials don’t lie — what the treatment has shown is a 148 percent increase in the optimization of the skin’s own natural elastin and a 159 percent increase in the optimization of the skin’s own natural collagen while using the product, according to the brand. At the same time, 100 percent of volunteers in the study agreed skin firmness improved after daily use, while 93 percent agreed skin looked smoother and younger after daily use.

    Reviewers are also thrilled with their results after using the Barrier Bioactive Treatment, with one calling it “overnight magic” and saying, “A little really goes a long way with this overnight treatment — the tiniest dollop on top of the hydrator at night and I wake up with skin that doesn’t just look great, but most importantly feels great.” They also appreciate how the product melts into the skin without leaving behind a sticky residue.

    Other users of the product love how hydrating and refreshing their complexion looks when they wake up in the morning and see such fast results. “I have been using this at night after I apply tretinoin. It has been a game-changer with my nightly routine. Most moisturizers leave your skin as it was as soon as you wash your face but the Barrier keeps it soft and supple and ready for the Resurfacing Compound,” one shopper commented.

    And one other five-star reviewer who notices dull, dry, crepey skin when the seasons change says, “This product solves its all. I love it and can not EVEN believe how well it plumps where my skin had become a wee ‘crepey’… Tip: I used it at 5 p.m. to freshen after a long day in drying AC… amazing refresh. Worth every penny. Get a subscription, you won’t regret it.”

    If you need some direction on how to use this product, we’ll give you a run-through (don’t worry, it’s quite simple). You’ll want to use this treatment as the last step in your skincare regimen, applying a generous amount before you hit the hay. The brand advises using it two to three nights per week in order to allow skin to have some time to regenerate in between applications.
