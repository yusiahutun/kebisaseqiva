    24 Sep 2024, 00:00

    Kylie Jenner is repping Sam Edelman shoes this March but her fans and followers are having none of it. The Kardashians star is known to collaborate with many different fashion brands. But, this time around, people aren’t convinced she’s ever worn anything by Sam Edelman.

    When it comes to the Kardashians, the Kar-Jenners are in high demand. Everything they do is a hot topic from the brands they’re the face of to the people they’re dating. Most of Kim Kardashian’s fans are obsessed with who she’s dating, while Kylie Jenner‘s followers are always keeping a keen eye on her moves in the business and fashion worlds.

    Kylie Jenner x Sam Edelman collab

    According to a Forbes interview with Sam Edelman, his brand is all about providing high-quality footwear at affordable prices.

    He and his wife, Libby, formed the brand in the early 2000s and have evolved to use AI to help create their comfort-focused shoes.

    The designing duo clearly have a top tier budget considering they’ve collaborated with the momager or all momager’s daughter’s – Miss Kylie Jenner – this March.

    Kylie’s ’empowering’ campaign

    Following Kylie’s show-stopping look at the Vanity Fair Oscars after-party, the 26-year-old shared her latest collaboration with the world.

    Kylie’s “empowering” Sam Edelman campaign went live on Instagram on March 11.

    The IG post shares some snaps of Kylie on set and more from behind the scenes of her iconic photoshoot.

    The caption reads: “For 20 years, Sam Edelman remains an emblem of timeless elegance and fashion. In honor of our anniversary, @kyliejenner steps out as the face of this empowering campaign.”

    View Instagram Post

    Edelman fans aren’t buying it

    While it seems both Sam Edelman and Kylie, and her momager Kris, are all super excited about the Hulu star’s latest fashion collab, many people who love the brand are expressing their total “disappointment” at the post.

    Taking to the comments section, one person wrote: “In all her years I’ve never seen her wear anything Sam Edelman. Does anyone on PR teams actually read through comment sections and listen to what women want to see?”

    Another asked: “How much did you pay her for this because we all know she does NOT wear this brand lol.”

    More went as far as to say: “I really like this brand. Why her!!! Makes me not wanna to purchase this brand now.”

    However, lots more jumped to Kylie’s defense, writing: “Come on people stop it….. Kylie isn’t the brand she is just the face of this campaign. Sam Edelman shoes are top notch and amazing!!!”
