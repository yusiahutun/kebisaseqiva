    In her latest Instagram post, Golden Globe winner Tracee Ellis Ross managed to make a serious case for self-care and prove that supposed imperfections (like gray hairs) don't need to be hidden.

    Ross's snapshot shows off her gray hair, pulled up into a topknot, and a statement-making coffee mug that says, "Hair care is self-care."

    Though the actress and businesswoman kept things pretty natural for the shot, it does feature searing, bright red-orange nail polish. Commenters loved the casual snapshot as well as Ross's mug, with a slew of fans asking where they could get their own. Ross hasn't offered up any insight, but she did encourage everyone to slow down and take some time for themselves.

    "A gentle start to this rainy Monday," she captioned the shot, which also featured Pattern, her haircare line, in the background.

    The shot only gave followers a peek at Ross's bathroom, but if you're looking for more, she recently offered a tour of her entire home in the latest spread for Shape.

    "What I've realized during this pandemic is that my entire home is my meditation space," she told the publication. She also opened up about her self-care routine, outside of taking baths.

    "One of the things that's been lovely to discover is how I care for myself and how I actively love myself," she added. "And I believe that love is an action: You get back what you put in."
