    Gregg Allman has found his seventh bride.

    The 64-year-old, who is a founding member of The Allman Brothers Band, is engaged to a 24-year-old woman named Shannon.

    'This time I'm really in love,' he said on CNN's Piers Morgan Tonight.

    Seventh time lucky: Gregg Allman revealed he is engaged to a 24-year-old blonde named Shannon during an interview on Piers Morgan Tonight

    Allman has been married six times, including a four year union with Cher during the Seventies.

    Their relationship produced a son, Elijah Blue, and Allman has four other children.

    His other ex wives include Shelley Kay Winters, singer Julie Bindas, Danielle J P Galiana, and Stacey Fountain.

    'This time I'm really in love': The musician introduced his fiancée for the first time

    Promotional trail: Gregg has released a new memoir My Cross to Bear

    Piers asked Allman: 'How does [Shannon] feel about becoming wife number seven?'

    'That's not what she's becoming,' Allman replied.

    'She's becoming wife number one. I don't have a wife, I haven't had one for years.'

    Wife No. 3: The star was married to Cher for four years during the Seventies

    Allman said he's only been in love with two of his six wives - but 'they didn't reciprocate.'

    The musican is promoting his new memoir My Cross to Bear, in which he details how drugs and money broke up the Allman Brothers Band in the summer of 1976.

    The group have reunited numerous times with various lineups in the 25 years since then.
