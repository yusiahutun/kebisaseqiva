    Even beautiful Hollywood actresses have their imperfections.

    Grey's Anatomy star Ellen Pompeo was out and about in Los Feliz, California yesterday and her open toe sandals showed off her rather unique feet.

    The 40-year-old actress appears to have a little extra than the regular person when it comes to the toe department - with six toes on either foot.

    Unusual anatomy: Ellen Pompeo was dressed casually as she walked to her car in Los Feliz yesterday - her leather flip flops showed off her slightly less than regular feet

    Count them out: Pompeo appears to have six toes on each foot

    Pompeo looked slim and trim as she walked to her car, dressed casually in a grey T-shirt and dark jeans rolled at the cuff.

    The mother-of-one is gearing up for her daughter Stella's first birthday on September 15.

    Stella's father is music producer, Chris Ivery, whom Pompeo married in 2007 after three years of dating.

    Pompeo says they were in no rush to have a child and it was important to them to wait until the time was completely right.

    Happy family: Pompeo married her husband, Chris Ivery in 2007, the couple's first child, Stella, was born September 15

    She told Allure magazine: 'It's a huge lifestyle change, and I take that very seriously.

    '[Chris and I] enjoy our life so much. We go to Europe and sleep in. So when I have the baby, I won't feel like we've missed any fun, reckless times together, like staying up all night drinking champagne and having sex until five in the morning and not having to get up until two in the afternoon.'

    Pompeo says she is loving motherhood and that her life couldn't be any better: 'I am over the moon,' the actress raves. 'Happiness is not that easy to achieve, but having a handsome husband, a beautiful baby, and a great job helps.'

    She recently revealed she is planning on leaving Grey's Anatomy, in which she plays the title character, Dr. Meredith Grey.

    'I think the world has had a lot of Meredith Grey, I haven’t said I’m moving on; I have said that when my contract is up after season eight, I will not renew.'

    And she has clear plans for her early retirement: 'I will raise my daughter, buy a house in Italy, learn Italian and teach it to my daughter,' she predicts.

    'I love buying houses and redoing them, and I’ll have that and my wonderful husband and my baby.'
