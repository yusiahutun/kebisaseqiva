    For someone who works in the restaurant and bar industry, Lisa Vanderpump always manages to keep it together -- but on Monday's episode of "Vanderpump Rules," we got to see her turn up.

    And it was amazing.

    During the new hour, Lisa, Tom Sandoval and Tom Schwartz scrambled to make sure their new West Hollywood bar -- TomTom -- would be ready in time for a big party they were contractually committed to throwing. They managed to cross every T and dot every I before guests made their way inside, where the happy tears were flowin'.

    Tears were flowing for Kristen Doute, too, only they weren't so happy. At the very last minute, Schwartz let her know that they were "over capacity" and that Lisa was "adamant" she not show up. Lisa made an exception for her other troublemakers -- Jax Taylor and James Kennedy -- because it was Jax's birthday, and because it was an opportunity for James to soberly DJ an event at someplace other than SUR.

    Toward the end of the evening, the Toms found Lisa sitting at a booth, doing that head bob we all know quite well. "Are you smashed?!" Schwartz asked with delight. LVP looked up at him with glazed eyes and said, "No, not at all!"

    (Cue footage of Lisa downing pink martinis just hours before, going, "Why are they so strong?")

    When Sandoval asked Lisa if she had had a good time, she let out the cutest, "I did!" and smiled big. The boys were over the moon.

    "You did?!" Sandoval said as he leaned in for a hug. "Look at how cute you are!"

    He later told the camera, "Lisa being drunk right now is such a huge compliment. She feels Schwartz and I are capable of handling this environment enough for her to get drunk!"

    Lisa smiled as she leaned in and said, "This is a starting point for you guys. This is the starting point for you to jump off."     A sober Lisa later praised her boys, telling the camera, "I'm so happy for Tom and Tom because everything's come to fruition, and they're really embracing it. This is exactly the vision that I had for them."
