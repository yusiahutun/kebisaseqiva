    The former best friends had a giggle with a new ad campaign

      How the tables have turned! Kim Kardashian was once Paris Hilton's assistant, with the BFFs being inseparable during the noughties. Then Kim found fame on and the friends were rumoured to have fallen out. Now, it seems the ladies have patched things up, giving their fans a giggle in the process. Paris dressed up as Kim for Kanye West's new campaign, and the internet has gone crazy for the photos!

      Photo credit: Instagram/ Paris Hilton

      Both Kim and Paris have shared the fun pictures on their Instagram pages. At first glance, it appears to be Kim advertising her husband's new line for Yeezy – but look a little closer and you'll see it is in fact her old friend Paris playing Kim's clone. Paris looks fantastic, with blue crop top and beige trousers and boots, with her hair in classic Kim style parted in the middle. Paris wrote: "So much fun being a #KimClone in the new #YeezySeason6campaign."

      Photo credit: Instagram/ Paris Hilton

      Fans of star loved the cheeky snaps, with one commenting: "She actually looks like Kim Kardashian!" Another wrote: "I literally thought it was Kim." A third said: "Omg I thought it's someone else, looks so different absolutely stunning." Over on Kim's Twitter page, she posted the same photos and captioned one: "The OG @ParisHilton #YEEZYSEASON6."

      Paris and Kim back in 2007

      Looks like Paris and Kim are on good terms once more if these snaps are anything to go by. Kim tweeted more photos from the advertising campaign, showing models including Abigail Ratchford, Madeleine Rose, Sami Miro, Sarah Snyder and Yovanna Ventura also dressed up as Kim. Now, when is Kim going to dress up as Paris?
