    1. Buckhead

    Buckhead is the uptown commercial and residential district of Atlanta, Georgia. It has a vibrant, rich history. Buckhead is the third-largest business district in Atlanta. It has some of the most beautiful mansions for sale in Atlanta and is a favourite among the city's elite.

    The neighbourhood is considered to be the pinnacle of luxury living. The neighbourhood has become a favourite among popular celebrities thanks to its lavish mansions, fine dining establishments, and high-end shopping boutiques. Homes are beautifully landscaped, and the neighbourhood features highly walkable streets.

    Most celebrities planning to purchase a home in Atlanta make their first stop in Buckhead. From palatial estates to modern state-of-the-art penthouses, the area offers a diverse variety of housing options. Some celebrities living in the posh neighbourhood include Josh Brolin and Steve Harvey.

    2. Peachtree Hills

    Peachtree is an affluent neighbourhood nestled in the heart of central Buckhead. It is one of the cosiest neighbourhoods in Atlanta. As you stroll through the neighbourhood, you'll be captivated by the timeless allure of the homes that line the picturesque streets. Homes in the neighbourhood are charming 1920s craftsman and bungalow styles.

    Walking along the streets, you will notice the beautifully landscaped yards and flower gardens gracing the sidewalk-lined streets. The neighbourhood is ideal for celebrities who prefer luxury homes in various architectural styles. Many celebrities have joined the ranks of people who call this peachy city home. They include Isla Fisher and Sacha Baron Cohen.

    McDonough is a city in Henry County, Georgia, United States. The city is renowned for its historic downtown district and picturesque surroundings. Its historic downtown square is a hub of activity, with local shops, restaurants, and events.
