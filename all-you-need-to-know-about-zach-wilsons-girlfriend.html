    Zach Wilson is an American football quarterback who plays for the New York Jets in the NFL. Wilson has been dating Nicolette Dellanno for several months, and if their social media posts are anything to go by, their bond is getting stronger.

    It made people realize the couple has gotten closer, making his fans curious about her. Here’s what you need to know about Zach Wilson’s girlfriend.

    Who Is Zach Wilson’s Girlfriend? 

    Zach Wilson’s girlfriend is Nicolette Dellanno. She is a TikTok star and model who is also active on Instagram. The gorgeous model’s Instagram page has over 40,000 followers, while her TikTok boasts over 200,000 followers. Her videos on the streaming platform usually get considerable likes and views.  

    She shares glamorous pictures and bikini shots of herself which her Instagram fans love. Aside from those, she also regularly shares views from photo shoots and hangouts with friends. In her posts, she often wears a necklace with the number two on it — Wilson’s Jets number.

    Given her dedication to their budding relationship, it is unsurprising that Wilson’s mother, Lisa, is one of her biggest fans. The older woman likes and comments on Dellanno’s glam pics, hinting at their remarkable relationship. 

    Zach Wilson and Nicolette Dellanno’s Relationship

    Wilson and Dellanno started dating while he was going through a difficult time off the field. Around that time, he made headlines for allegedly having an affair with his mom’s best friend. Wilson’s ex-girlfriend Abbey Gile made the allegation. 

    She reportedly made the shocking claim after her new love interest Dax Milne posted a snap of the two on Instagram. Gile claimed: “He (Wilson) was sleeping with his mom’s best friend… that’s the real homie hopper.”

    In one photo, Dellanno was dressed in a highly revealing swimsuit, and in another shot, Wilson was seen holding a drink while standing on a yacht. The photos quickly went viral on social media, sparking much discussion.

    Some people criticized Dellanno for her much too revealing outfit, while others praised her for her confidence. Fortunately, Zach Wilson’s girlfriend and he have not taken the statements to heart. As such, they have not responded. 

    What Does Zach Wilson’s Girlfriend Do?

    Fans are glad Wilson has found something great with Dellanno. He has been spending much time with her as he prepares to return to the NFL. Experts have predicted yet another challenging year for him in New York, as he is now back up to the team’s star Aaron Rodgers.
