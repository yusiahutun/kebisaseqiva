    Brooke Lynette – famously known as Brookelyn – has been giving her 593,000 fans on Instagram a glimpse into the future by posting sneak previews of her latest photoshoot.

    The blonde bombshell shot to fame earlier this year when her hilarious videos racked up over 150m views on social media.

    And the Insta model – who is currently the most viewed female in the UK – has an army of 1.7million followers on Facebook.

    But now, the sex bomb is hoping to step up her game by selling her first ever merchandise next month.

    Talking about her sexy 2018 calendar, Brookelyn exclusively told the Daily Star Online: “I have no idea what I want to get out of doing this calendar.

    “I’ve never sold any merchandise. I don’t even know if it will sell. I’m just going to see how it goes.”

    The Scots girl – who lives in London – flew out to LA last month to begin shooting for her calendar with her best friend @sukeyselva.

    She added: “It’s a huge step for me, people are like to me ‘what do you mean a calendar?’… My friend’s are like ‘are people gonna buy it?’

    And it turns out that Brookelyn – who calls herself “That Video Girl” – has pretty much gone global now.

    “Ideally I’d want to sell my calendars worldwide, my fans are worldwide. So it’s not fair to just to sell it to one area,” she said.

    “The US is on top and UK is second with where my fans are, but I get a lot of people from Germany, India and even the Middle East.

    “And for my first one I’d like to go all out with the sexy shots, lingerie and implied nude.”

    Earlier this month, the Insta babe posted a video called “How to get rid of a one night stand” which was viewed over 490,000 times on Instagram.

    But now her latest clip "Will you drop your towel for $200" not only racked up over 30 million views over her Instagram, Facebook and Twitter pages, it was her most viewed video.

    Celebrating her success, Brookelyn told us: “I grew 150k in the space of a week from that video and I grew like 60k on Instagram in a week which is a lot.

    “I was buzzing, I put up that video thinking ‘oh this video is alright, it probably won’t do anything’ but when I saw how many views it racked up I was like ‘what is going on?’”
