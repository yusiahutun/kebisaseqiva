    TikTok is one of the biggest social media apps. Despite the commonality of ideologies with other social media apps, the platform has distinct or differently packaged features. One of the most used functionalities on the app is the live feature. It is interesting watching someone live streaming. However, if you are only interested in the stream, you might find the comments that flash on the TikTok live a nuisance. So, you should educate yourself on how to hide comments on TikTok live as a viewer if you want peace of mind.

    How do I turn off comments when watching TikTok live? If you have stumbled across one of your favourite creator's streams and find the live comments scrolling over the video disturbing, you will be glad to know that you can get rid of them and enjoy the stream. It is quite easy, and you will be done in a few simple steps.

    How to hide comments on TikTok live as a viewer

    You can hide comments in TikTok as a viewer in three easy ways. This way, you do not have to worry about the distracting comments getting in your way while you enjoy your favourite video.

    Hit the clear display button

    The first and easiest way of hiding comments while watching a TikTok live is to hit the "Clear Display" button. You can access the button from the share icon on your phone's lower screen. After clicking the share button, tap "Clear Display" on the dialog box.

    On top of this, some Android users have reported that they have the button, while some iPhone users report that they cannot locate it. So, if you cannot locate the button, you can use the other method of clearing the live messages from TikTok live.

    Swipe away from the side

    If you are wondering how to hide chat while watching TikTok live on Android, you will be glad to know that it is as easy as swiping. This method is the most common and, of course, easiest.

    You can eliminate the live chat by swiping right from the left side of your phone's screen. Once you start swiping, the chat window will start moving and will be eliminated once you finish swiping. It cannot come back even if people continue typing more messages during the live.

    If you want to bring the message feature back, you must swipe from the opposite direction. Everything will return to the way it was.

    Tap and hold and then tap “Clear Display”

    Do you want to know how to hide chat on TikTok live on iPhone? The good thing with TikTok is that most of its functionalities work the same way on all smartphone operating systems. Whatever you do on Android will probably be the same for iPhone. So, how do you do it?

    You can use this method if neither of the two previous methods works. Tap the live video and hold your two fingers down for a few seconds. The action will prompt the phone to open a new window with the "Clear Display" button. Click it to clear the display and hide the chat.

    Can you hide comments on TikTok live by default?

    Unfortunately, for viewers, there is no way of hiding live chats by default, and you must do it manually every time. Although it is frustrating that such a giant app doesn't have this function by default, the good thing is that doing it is not a big deal.

    What remains on TikTok live after hiding the comments?

    Once you get rid of the comments, you will only be left with the creator’s name at the top of the screen and the live video. There will also be an exit button.

    Why can't I hide comments on TikTok live?

    The comments appear by default every time you open a TikTok live, and it appears there is no way to deactivate it from settings permanently. Therefore, you must use manual means to eliminate the comments, like swiping from left to right or using the "Clear Display" option.

    How to turn comments off on TikTok live as a moderator?

    If you are the one (moderator) hosting the live stream, you can turn off comments if you find the feature annoying. And you can use the following steps:

    • If you are using an iPhone or an Android device, tap on the “+” icon in the middle of the screen. The action will bring the option to go live.
    • Click on the go live option to start the live stream.
    • Click on the setting gear icon on the screen to get to the live setting screen.
    • Access the comments setting, toggle the "Allow Comments" and change it from green to grey. This will turn off the comments section in your live stream.
    • Remember that if you go live again, you will have to change the settings once more, as the comments settings only work for the present live stream.
