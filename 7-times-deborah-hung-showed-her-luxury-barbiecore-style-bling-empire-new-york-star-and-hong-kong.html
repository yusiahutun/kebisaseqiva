    Whether fuchsia or baby pink, the Barbie aesthetic was a clear fashion trend that gained momentum in 2022 … and looks to continue in 2023. Better known as “Barbiecore”, it was designer Valentino’s autumn/winter 2022-2023 collection that solidified the trend with fashionistas and Hollywood A-listers quickly donning the signature colour.

    Valentino’s creative director Pierpaolo Piccioli stripped down his fashion palette to a single hue, saying in a statement that he considers pink to be a “manifestation of the unconscious and liberation from the need for realism”.

    As the bold hue is expected to dominate the red carpet this year with the release of the Barbie film in July, Deborah Hung has been front and centre of the pink squad. The Bling Empire: New York star, who is married to Hong Kong millionaire Stephen Hung, has always unashamedly been a fan of pink. Her wardrobe only consists of designer pink outfits with pieces from Louis Vuitton, Chanel, Dior and Fendi, per Asian media.

    Not only does she only wear designer, Hung shares a close relationship with well-known designers too. In an interview, she said she loves haute couture at fashion weeks as the pieces showcased “are extraordinary”.

    Which luxury brands will launch BTS members as their global ambassadors next?

    The beauty also said she is close with Donatella Versace, and is known to hang out with the designer backstage ahead of runway shows. At one Dolce & Gabbana event, Hung even said the store was opened just for her as she had been travelling and didn’t have a dress to wear.

    Just one look at the Mexican-born Hong Kong-raised socialite’s Instagram page, and you’ll see all the luxury drip you could ever imagine.

    Here are seven of her most outstanding “Barbiecore” looks …

    1. Channelling Elle Woods

    Hung returned to Hong Kong in January and the Elle Woods energy was turned up to 11. She wore a baby pink tweed jacket with a Chanel pearl choker. Of course, her fur baby was also in tow.

    2. Couture with Faith Connexion

    Hung was at a private dinner at Haiku Miami with her husband and fellow Bling Empire star Kane Lim in February 2022, wearing a dress by Paris-based design house Faith Connexion. The fashion house is a collective of designers and artists who aim to create a new kind of fashion for affluent millennials.

    3. Dior’s signature rose

    In March 2022, Hung visited Dior’s 30 Montaigne flagship store. She wore a pink minidress made of satin, which focused on a Dior key inspiration – the rose. She paired the look with Mach & Mach crystal heels and a mini Lady Dior bag in nylon.

    4. Fashion week ensemble

    Hung wore her signature colour at the Paris Haute Couture Fashion Week 2022. The crystal-embellished top is shaped in a bow. Along with a flared pink skirt and studded handbag, the outfit made her look dreamy indeed.

    5. Casual in Chanel

    It’s no secret that Hung likes to travel. Her Instagram page is filled with images of her travelling by private jet to exotic locations. In this snapshot from December 2020, she looks casual but still stylish, wearing a bright pink Chanel cardigan with snow in the background.

    6. Rami Kadi gown

    Hung has been a long-time lover of Maison Rami Kadi. His avant-garde garments are the perfect choices for statement pieces. Hung looked like a princess in this pink gown with bead work and feathers.

    7. Princess life in pink

    In this image posted in October last year, Hung looked every bit like she came straight out of a fairy tale. With a fitted pink bodice and flowing skirt, she added silver shoes and a tiara to finish the look. She’s seated in a custom pink Rolls-Royce.
