    London is looking more star-studded than ever with Prince Harry and Meghan Markle's wedding guests descending upon Windsor Castle. Angelina Jolie's arrival into Heathrow airport has sparked rumors she'll be in attendance, George and Amal Clooney have just arrived looking endlessly chic. One of Markle's best friends, Quantico actress Priyanka Chopra, just arrived to the ceremony in a custom Vivienne Westwood lavender suit that's equal parts First Lady, Ladies who Lunch and Royal Wedding Guest perfection.

    Yesterday, Chopra got her last minute preparations in, running errands in the Battersea neighborhood. The actress appeared to make a stop at Philip Treacy's London studio, picking up her custom fascinator for the wedding, which matched her suit perfectly. The look featured an asymmetrical neckline, crystal buttons and a cocktail-length skirt.

    Said Priyanka of her dear friend prior to the wedding, "I've known Meghan for three years and I’m so happy the way the world has responded to her because as long as I’ve known her … she is a relatable young woman of the world today,” she recently told Us Weekly. “She’s that girl who thinks out of the box. She has always been an activist. She has always had a strong voice. She’s an ambitious girl of today. I think the world needs strong role models like Meghan. I think, I hope, Meghan will end up being one of those people.”

    She's previously gushed over Meghan in her TIME Most Influential People profile: "This ever-smiling, strong free spirit found her prince, fell in love and in turn made a cynical world believe in fairy tales again. But more than anything else, Meghan is an important influencer in a world that needs strong public figures to respect and look up to. People the world can be inspired by. Meghan, standing shoulder to shoulder with Harry, will be a princess for the people."

    Shutterstock

    On her look, Philip Treacy has designed custom headwear for Catherine, Duchess of Cambridge, Meghan Markle and other famous faces like Victoria Beckham, Lady Gaga and Sarah Jessica Parker. He's also crafted hats for Princesses Eugenie and Beatrice of York, who shocked royal and fashion fans with their over-the-top toppers at Will & Kate's 2011 nuptials. Treacy will undoubtedly be responsible for a solid array of the spectacular toppers on display tomorrow at Windsor Castle's St. George's Cathedral.

    While Chopra is one of Markle's closest friends she will not have an official role in the wedding. Rather than opt for a traditional American bridal party, Meghan and Harry have opted for traditional British bridesmaids and page boys–which means children only. Meghan opted not to elect a maid of honor for her big day, but she'll have her mother and (most likely) her stylist Jessica Mulroney at her side. Harry, expectedly, did choose a best man–his brother, William, the Duke of Cambridge will be supporting him tomorrow at the altar and his father, Prince Charles, will escort Meghan down the aisle.
