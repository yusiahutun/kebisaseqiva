    The breakout star from Netflix’s DC Comics Titans Teagan Croft is still a child and has gained massive attraction in a short period of time. Australian actress showcased amazing performance in the role of Rachel Roth in the series and also known for playing the title character in the 2016 science-fiction film The Osiris Child.

    Early life

    Teagan was born on 23 April 2004 in Sydney, New South Wales, Australia to father Tims Croft and mother Rebecca McNamee-Croft. Her mother is an actress who has worked in films and television, one of them being titled Acheron. Teagan has two younger sisters named Sage and Aubrey Croft. Her aunts Penny McNamee and Jessica McNamee are famous Australian actresses who have appeared in many notable works. In 2016, her family relocated to Chicago, Illinois as Teagan was mostly working in America.

    Teagan is an avid book reader and enjoys listening to music while she is spending some quality time with herself. Currently, Teagan is enrolled in high school though details regarding the same are not known or published by the actress. there are no details regarding her stand on her further senior education.

    Teagan Croft Career

    Croft’s acting career began at a very young age when she played the part of Scout Finch in the theatrical adaption of To Kill a Mockingbird at the age of 9 which was based on the novel of the same name. She has garnered enough attention from the producers which led Croft to be cast in the Australian film The Osiris Child: Science Fiction Volume One, which marked her debut in the film industry. She played the role of Indi Sommerville, daughter of Kane Sommerville.

    The same year, she appeared in the television series Home and Way which marked her television debut, Teagan appeared as a supporting character named Bella Loneragan. In 2018, Teagan was cast to play the lead role of Rachel Roth in the American superhero television series Titans. The first two seasons of the show were aired on DC Universe and the current ongoing season of the show is being aired on HBO MAX. In 2020, she played the same role and appeared in the television series Legends of Tomorrow.

    Teagan is the executive producer of a yet-to-be-released short film titled Bella and Bernie where she will also be playing a role. Her mother, Rebecca is also the executive producer of the film which is set to be released in 2021.

    Social media

    Like any other young teenager, Teagan is also active on social media platforms. Her presence is widely known as she is a verified Instagram user with 634k followers. Moreover, she even has a Twitter account with 27.2k followers and a Facebook page with 12k followers. On all her social media bio, she goes by mentioning “I’m an actor!!!”.
