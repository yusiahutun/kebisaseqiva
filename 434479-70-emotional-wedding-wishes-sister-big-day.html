    Every parent's dream is to see their children grow into adults and start their families, but the situation is a little different with siblings. If it were up to them, they would rather remain together into old age. This kind of attachment makes it difficult to say goodbye to a sister who is getting married. As a result, you will find yourself writing emotional wedding wishes for your sister as she goes to start a new life.

    Weddings are meant to be happy ceremonies, but most siblings tend to be overcome by emotions and find themselves crying because they are happy or because they will miss the one getting married. So to leave a lasting impression on your sister’s heart, your congratulations on the marriage card should be lovely and touching. This will put a smile on your sister’s face and make her give you a tight hug.
