    Inside of Antony Starr’s Career

    Antony Starr started his career from the American fantasy television series, Xena: Warrior Princess where he played the role of Mesas for an episode of Hooves and Harlots in 1995.     Starr made his appearance in the Brad McGann’s movie, In My Father’s Den where he played the role of Gareth in 2004. He then starred as Jeff in the New Zealand biographical sports drama movie, The World’s Fastest Indian in 2005. He also featured in several other movies such as After the Waterfall, Wish You Were Here, and many others. His most recent role is in the television series The Boys where he is portraying the main role as The Homelander.
