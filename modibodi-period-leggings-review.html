      But what exactly period leggings, we hear you ask? Well, HELLO! has all of the answers about this environmentally friendly alternative to sanitary products…

      What are the period pants and period leggings?

      Period pants and period leggings are products which take the place of tampons, panty liners, pads, and other incontinence products. They claim to help protect against leakage whilst on your period, and leave you feeling dry and protected.

      In the UK, a lot of period products contain plastic, which contribues to 200,000 tonnes of landfill waste every year, plus ocean issues when sanitary products are flushed down toilets. The beauty of period pants and period leggings is that they can be reused, so are great for the environment. After wear, simply pop them into the wash and let them air dry.

      Is it OK to wear leggings on your period?

      Totally! Period leggings with the right protection can be perfect for those who suffer with heavy periods or even bladder leakage. Some leggings, like the Modibodi ones, have stain, odour, and bacteria-resistant lining for leak-proof action, too.

      Should I wear period leggings whilst working out?

      Period proof leggings are an especially great solution for workout lovers, as they help to give you control over your flow, whether you’re practising some gentle yoga - or doing more hardcore deadlifts.
