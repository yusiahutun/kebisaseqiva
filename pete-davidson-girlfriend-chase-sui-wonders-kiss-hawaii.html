    Pete Davidson and Chase Sui Wonders Kiss on Romantic Hawaii Getaway

    09 Jul 2024, 00:00

    Pete Davidson and Chase Sui Wonders's relationship may be getting more and more serious, as the new couple has just jetted off to Hawaii for a romantic and relaxing getaway.

    On Tuesday, the two were seen enjoying a sunny day in Kauai, during which they shared various sweet kisses.

    In one shot, Davidson is seen sitting on a park bench with Wonders, standing over him, leaning down to kiss him.

    The comedian looked casual in a grayish-blue hoodie over a white T-shirt, khaki shorts, a gray beanie, black sunglasses, and long white Nike socks with white slides. Wonders wore a black spaghetti-strap crop top with a plaid schoolgirl skirt, black socks, and black sneakers. She accessorized with red sunglasses and a pearl necklace.

    Also during the day, Wonders was pictured going for a splash in the ocean. She wore a colorful floral bikini in green, red, pink, and purple, with white details, and accessorized with her same pearl necklace, a silver watch, and pearl drop earrings.

    The couple has been staying at the five-star 1 Hotel Hanalei Bay in the island, BAZAAR.com has learned.

    Wonders and Davidson have been dating for a few weeks now. Late last month, they attended the Daytona 500 (a NASCAR Cup Series motor race) in Florida together. And back in January, they were spotted enjoying a date at Universal Studios Hollywood.

    Romance rumors first sparked in early January when the two were seen grabbing food in New York City together and later sat courtside at a New York Rangers hockey game against the Toronto Maple Leafs.

    The stars met on the set of Bodies Bodies Bodies, A24's dark comedy film that premiered at South by Southwest on March 14, 2022, and was theatrically released in the United States on August 5.
