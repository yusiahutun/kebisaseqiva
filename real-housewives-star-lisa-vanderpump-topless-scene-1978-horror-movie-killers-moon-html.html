    As a reality show star on the Real Housewives of Beverly Hills, there are few areas of her life which she hasn't shared with the general public.

    But perhaps Lisa Vanderpump has been trying to forget the topless scene she filmed for 1978 flop horror film Killer's Moon.

    A still from the little remembered 1978 British movie featuring the one-time actress shows the star - then 18-years-old - lying on a table with her shirt undone.

    Scroll down for video

    Exposed: Lisa Vanderpump filmed a topless scene while starring as a schoolgirl in 1978 horror film Killer's Moon

    A dog's life: Lisa, pictured earlier this month, has moved on from acting

    Lisa plays a schoolgirl in the film which sees a group of stranded schoolgirls terrorised by escaped asylum inmates.

    The topless scene shows Lisa being attacked by one of the escapees.

    Lisa, who has been married to businessman and restaurateur Ken Todd for more than 30 years, began acting as a child and appeared in a smattering of TV dramas and films throughout the 1970s and 1980s.

    According to Star magazine who published the photograph, the Killer Moon part was only the third role Lisa had taken on after appearing in just a film and BBC TV series.

    'I think I made the choice to kind of give it up before it gave me up,' Lisa recently told
    ABC of her acting career which also included an appearance in Baywatch Nights.

    'It was fun. I don't think that will be on my epitaph.'

    Following her attempt at an acting career, the star moved to Beverly Hills with Ken and made her Real Housewives debut in 2010, followed by spin-off series Vanderpump Rules about her restaurant Sur.

    Fledgling actress: Lisa in a scene from the horror movie which went on to gain a cult following

    Before the terror: Another scene from the film's trailer shows Lisa with the escaped inmates and her schoolgirl friends

    Star of the horror show: Lisa (right) on the cover of the film's DVD

    Lisa recently took part in TV show Dancing with the Stars with Gleb Savchenko but the couple were eliminated in the fourth week of competition.

    'I've been stretched in places that I didn't know existed, she told of her experience on the hit show.

    'I just thought, "Wow, it's such a challenge," she said of her decision to appear on the programme. 'There were moments that I thought, "Have I lost my mind?'"

    She is clearly enjoying some aspects of her new found fame and the opportunities it has brought her. 

    'I would be lying if I didn't say that every celebrity aspect of [being a reality TV star] isn't beneficial,' she said. 'For instance, I have written a book... before I had a book offer.

    'It's the same book I could have written two or three years ago, but nobody would have bought it.

    'I'm the same person I was three years ago,' Lisa added. 'Before I opened my life, and it's just a plethora of opportunities.'
