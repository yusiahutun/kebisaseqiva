    Kandi Burruss has been a popular name in the TV industry for quite some time now. A member of the popular girl band called Xscape, Kandi first shot into the limelight for writing hit songs for shows such as TLC’s No Scrubs. Later, she also made her appearance on The Real Housewives of Atlanta.

    Kandi’s most successful business to date has to be her joint restaurant venture Old Lady Gang, which she opened up with help from her husband Todd and her mother and aunts. After the huge success of OLG, Kandi has finally gotten a brand new TV show, Bravo’s Kandi & The Gang. The new reality TV, which premiered on Bravo TV on March 6th, 2022, will give us the look at the personal and professional lives of the staff at OLG Atlanta restaurant.

    Brandon Black, the manager at OLG, is also one of the cast members of Kandi & The Gang. Learn about Brandon’s age, job, love life, and more in the wiki article below.

    Brandon Black On Kandi & The Gang

    For Kandi, Old Lady Gang is more than just a restaurant; it’s a medium to employ her loved ones and keep them happy. In the show Kandi & The Gang, viewers will get to see Kandi and her husband Todd come up “with a plan for strong leadership and a staff shakeup to bring the original OLG back to its former glory.”

    While the southern restaurant culture is all about wholesome family traditions, the show shows us how tricky it can be to work with a large family. The trailer for the show featured some delicious southern food and a whole lot of family drama. We can expect coworker clashing, Kandi’s family members fighting with the OLG staff, and some hookups too in Kandi & The Gang.

    As told earlier, Brandon Black is the manager at OLG. As a manager, Brandon oversees the staff, their schedules, and handles any issues that may arise during the day-to-day operation at OLG. Known as the “workplace flirt,” Brandon recently got into a relationship with a co-worker-turned-fling. However, with Philip’s arrival to bring the restaurant back on track, Brandon needs to maintain some level of professionalism in the workplace.

    Brandon made a Facebook post on February 11, 2022, and revealed that the whole show, Kandi and the Gang, was an experience to be remembered. He also asked his fans and followers to tune into Bravo TV on March 6th for the premiere of Kandi & the Gang.

    Recently, through an Instagram post, Brandon appreciated the production team’s work and posted pictures with them. He wrote, “Dopest Production Team, I’m grateful to have met them! LIZZIE!!! DOUG!!! COURTNEY!!,” in the caption.

    Brandon Black Net Worth

    As of this writing, Brandon Black’s net worth was reported to be under $250 thousand.

    Aside from being the manager at OLG Atlanta, he is also the founder of Twelve Thirty, according to his LinkedIn handle. Founded in April 2014, Twelve Thirty “is an entertainment firm providing services in event planning, promotions, and artist management.”

    Through Twelve Thirty, Brandon has been consistently making contributions in promoting Atlanta’s nightlife scene and independent music artists by providing enough exposure to them. Twelve Thirty also “volunteers heavily within the Atlanta Metro area and with Atlanta Public Schools.”

    In the future, Twelve Thirty aims to create a space for youth to have access to different opportunities and programs.

    Brandon completed his education at Georgia State University. He studied Public Policy at Georgia State.

    Is Brandon Black On Instagram?

    Brandon Black is on Instagram as @planblack. He had 6.8 thousand followers as of March 2022. You can find him on Facebook as well.

    Brandon Black Age

    During the filming of Bravo’s Old Lady Gang, Brandon Black’s age was 33 years old.

    Brandon Black Height

    Coming to his physical stats, Brandon stands tall at a height of 6 feet 2 inches.

    Is Brandon Black Dating Anyone?

    To talk about Brandon Black’s personal life, as we already know, he is the “workplace flirt” at Old Lady Gang. His romance with Dom’Unique Variety was teased on the trailer for OLG.

    Lately, his growing flirtationship with Dom’Unique has become the talk of OLG. Talking about Dom’Unique, she is “the self-proclaimed “day one” bartender at OLG and dancer who has performed with Kandi on the “Welcome to the Dungeon” Tour and even with thee hot girl, Megan Thee Stallion.”

    It is only a matter of time before we know how things will work out for Brandon with Dom’Unique.
