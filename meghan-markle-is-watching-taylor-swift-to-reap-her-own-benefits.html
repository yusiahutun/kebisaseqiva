    Meghan Markle is 'watching Taylor Swift' to reap her own benefits

    Taylor Swift is killing the game at the moment, and the Swifties are loving her more than ever, including Meghan Markle, as some claim she’s watching the music star to ‘claim some of her universal love.’

    Meghan Markle and Prince Harry recently traveled on a private jet to Katy Perry’s Las Vegas Show with an array of Hollywood stars, as one podcaster claims she’s ‘eager’ to ‘recreate’ Taylor Swift‘s iconic girl gang.

    Meghan Markle ‘desperate to have same type of visual’ as Taylor Swift

    The couple left the royal family in 2020, and have since moved over to their $15 million LA mansion and are now ‘living the celebrity lifestyle,’ some would claim.

    Royal commentator, and host of the To Die For podcast, Kinsey Schofield’s opinion states Meghan is “watching Taylor Swift” in hopes of receiving the same amount of love.

    “Meghan Markle has been watching Taylor Swift. Meghan loves these visuals of her girl gang,” Schofield claimed to Sky News Australia.

    “She is desperate to have the same type of visual,” she continued. “So yes, she’s going to jump on a private plane despite preaching we all need to watch our environmental footprint.”

    The Duchess of Sussex was also seen alongside music sensation Celine Dion, and the Kinsey it’s because “she’s trying to claim some of that universal love that Taylor has.”

    Despite touring the world, the Love Story singer still has time to hang out with her girl gang. She was spotted out in NYC with Selena Gomez and Sophie Turner last weekend before heading of to perform in Argentina.

    Meghan has an array of celebrity friends, with the likes of Serena Williams and Oprah Winfrey showing up as guests at her wedding.

    George and Amal Clooney were also on the list, however, it was reported Meg and Harry were allegedly ‘snubbed’ from their gala recently.

    Orlando Bloom ‘feels bad’ about Prince Harry role

    Orlando Bloom was also at his partner’s Las Vegas show, along with their daughter, Daisy who made her first public appearance.

    From what royal commentator Kinsey understands, she claims to Sky News Australia that it’s “Orlando Bloom trying to initiate the friendship,” between himself, Katy, Harry, and Meghan.

    She claims he feels “guilty” about his portrayal as Harry in the very short-lived HBO TV comedy, The Prince.

    However, speaking on Andy Cohen’s Watch What Happens Live in 2021, creator Gary Janetti said the Prince had a ‘sense of humor’ about it.

    “I don’t know if Harry has said anything to Orlando about it, but I know before the show premiered, he was aware that Orlando was doing it. And he seemed to have a sense of humor about it.”
