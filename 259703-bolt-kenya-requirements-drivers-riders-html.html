    Cars accepted by Bolt Kenya

    There are specific requirements for cars accepted by Bolt Kenya that you should know before registering your vehicle on the platform. The vehicle year of manufacture must be:

    • From 2009 onwards in Nairobi
    • From 2008 onwards in Mount Kenya area, Kakamega, and Kisumu
    • From 2007 onwards in Mombasa

    Bolt Kenya also accepts Boda Boda drivers in case you want to operate your motorcycle on this platform.

    How do I become a Bolt driver in Kenya?

    Subsequent to providing the above records and ensuring that you meet all the stated requirements, you must also sign up on their platform to create a driver account. You will be required to upload all the necessary documents during the Bolt sign up process. Finally, upon successful affirmation as a driver, you will need to visit their office in Nairobi for training at Azure Towers, 2nd Floor, Lantana Road, Westlands on any weekday at 11:00 am.

    How long does it take to become a Bolt driver?

    Submitting your data takes approximately 5 minutes. After that, you will get a 30-minute training in their office.
