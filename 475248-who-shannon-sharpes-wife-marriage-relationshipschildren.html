    Who is Shannon Sharpe?

    The former NFL player was born on 26 June 1968 in Chicago, Illinois, United States. He is 54 years old as of 2022. Shannon’s parents are Pete Sharpe and Mary Alice Dixon. His parents parted ways when he was only three months old. Unfortunately, his father died of cancer when he was in eighth grade. He has two siblings, an older brother, Sterling, and a sister Sherra.

    Sterling is a former NFL wide receiver and five-time all-pro receiver for the Green Bay Packers. Shannon's athletic skills were apparent from an early age, and he took part in various sports such as basketball and football. However, despite participating in multiple sports while in school, he ultimately focused solely on football.

    His big break came in 1990 after being drawn up in the seventh round by the Denver Broncos. He was an NFL tight end for ten years before signing as a free agent without restrictions with the Baltimore Ravens in 2000. He retired from the NFL after a 14-year career as the tight end with the most catches, yards, and touchdowns. He became a sports analyst after retiring.
