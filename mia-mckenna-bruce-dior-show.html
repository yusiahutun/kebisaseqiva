    It has already been quite the year for Mia McKenna-Bruce. The actress has been taking home awards left, right and centre – including the prestigious EE BAFTA Rising Star Award this past Sunday – for her incredibly moving and raw performance in the film, How To Have Sex. And now, the actress is attending her very first Paris Fashion Week – and she could not be enjoying the ride more.

    "I'm very excited," she tells me from the make-up chair, as she gets ready for her very first Dior show. "We're just getting glammed now, it's all happening!"

    Getty Images

    For such an occasion as this, the actress chose to keep things classic, but with a little French flair. "My look feels very Parisian," she says. "I love the femininity and structure of it, it's very elegant, but cool and modern at the same time."

    Below, see what McKenna-Bruce had to say about her love of Dior, her most memorable fashion moment so far and why she loves getting dressed up.

    Please talk us through your look – what drew you to this particular outfit?

    "For the show, I'm wearing a white ribbed tank top with a pleated skirt, a Lady D-Joy bag and a trench coat draped over my shoulders. A trench coat is the perfect wardrobe staple. My look feels very Parisian – I love the femininity and structure of it, it's very elegant, but cool and modern at the same time."

    How do you decide which brands you want to work with?

    "I've worked quite a lot with my stylist Holly [White] to play around with different brands and silhouettes to see where we can build from. We are always discovering new exciting brands to try as well as those highly established ones, which keeps the whole process really fun and exciting."

    What makes Dior such a great fit for you and your personal style?

    "With Dior, we found that Maria Grazia [Chiuri]'s classic silhouette of a super cinched waist really works for me. I'm very petite, so that detail is quite flattering for me. I love the mix of Dior's feminine and androgynous aesthetic, it feels authentic to me so it's such a great fit."

    How involved do you tend to be with the styling process?

    "It's definitely been a journey, I started out with no idea of the industry and how everything all works. But Holly has been amazing at keeping me informed and involved. We've been working really well together, so it's been so fun. Our relationship started when we first had a meeting a while back, I was on a job, so we spoke over Zoom and instantly got on really well. Then the Cannes Film Festival was approaching, so we reached out to her, I was six months pregnant at that point. She absolutely smashed it, so we've been on that fun journey together ever since."

    Darren Gerrish

    What kind of fashion makes you feel your most confident?

    "Something that has a structure to it works perfectly for me, or that accentuated waist I mentioned earlier makes me feel confident."

    How has your style evolved over the years?

    "It's one extreme to the other with me, I'm either wearing my pyjamas or I'm wearing Dior! The fashion I wore when I was pregnant was amazing. But obviously, we had a big bump to work with, so nowadays there's definitely more options available to us."

    Darren Gerrish

    What has been your most memorable fashion moment in recent years and why?

    "Wow, there's been quite a few, but it probably has to be Cannes for its red carpet. I wore this beautiful red Carolina Herrera dress that was just absolutely incredible. The festival was something I was quite worried about as I was quite heavily pregnant, all while having this huge moment in Cannes. The Herrera dress was the first one I tried on, and it was just absolutely perfect."

    Whose style have you always admired and why?

    "I adore Sofia Richie's style, I love everything she wears on Instagram. She's pregnant at the moment – she's so chic and always looks so great."

    What is your ideal getting-ready set-up?

    "Something very chill, with snacks on hand and coffee for sure. Right now, I've got some croissants on the go, which feels very Parisian."

    Darren Gerrish

    When do you feel most beautiful?

    "I feel the most beautiful simply when I feel confident. It's in moments like this, when there has been a whole team of people helping me get ready. It definitely takes a village, which makes me feel amazing and makes such a difference. Even seeing the photographs after an event where you've felt super confident, helps you hold yourself differently."

    Darren Gerrish

    Fashion has a reputation for being frivolous, but why does what we wear matter?

    "Fashion matters because it expresses who you are. It's almost like putting on this second skin. The experience I've had I've had with Holly reminds me how important it is to have fun with fashion, as you can lean into expressing different parts of your personality, as well as what makes you feel confident and beautiful. Different looks can make you feel different things, it's like being in character."

    Darren Gerrish
