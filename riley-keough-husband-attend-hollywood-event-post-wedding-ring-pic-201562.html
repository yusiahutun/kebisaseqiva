    Riley Keough, Husband Attend Hollywood Event Post-Wedding: Ring Pic

    12 Aug 2024, 00:00

    Stepping out — in her blue suede shoes! For newlyweds Riley Keough and her husband Ben Smith-Petersen, a far-flung honeymoon will apparently have to wait. Just one day after saying “I do” in Napa, Calif., the couple attended Louis Vuitton’s Series 2 Exhibition event in L.A. on Thursday, Feb. 5.

    The Magic Mike actress, 25, wore a multicolored frock for the couple’s first outing as husband and wife, and coordinated in blue suede shoes. (Adorably enough, her grandfather Elvis Presley made the ladies swoon with his trademark classic “Blue Suede Shoes” in the mid-’50s.) Keough also showed off her new sparkly wedding band. 

    An insider tells Us Weekly that Lisa Marie Presley’s daughter was greeted by fellow actress Juno Temple at the exhibit, who was over the moon about the nuptials.

    “Congratulations, congratulations!” Temple was overhead saying. Keough seemed just as excited to tell another friend about the wedding. As for if it feels different to be married? “Not yet!” she told her pal. Smith-Petersen added that “it’s very exciting.”
