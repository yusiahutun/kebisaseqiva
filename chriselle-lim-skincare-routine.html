    Influencer and Youtuber Chriselle Lim is pregnant with her second child, but that doesn't mean she lets her anti-aging skin routine fall to the wayside. Some common skincare ingredients, like rx retinoids, aren't safe to use while pregnant. Some women switch to an all-natural regime, but Lim just made a few tweaks to her routine to amplify her pregnancy glow—safely. "I swapped out some products that had a little bit more chemicals for more natural ingredients, just for the baby," she says.

    Shop Chriselle's Routine

    Lim starts with an eye makeup remover. She soaks a cotton pad and presses on her eyes to get every trace of mascara and shadow off. "I'm all about double cleansing, especially if you're wearing foundation, powder, all that good stuff, and you're running around all day," she says. "Most likely your pores are going to be a little clogged up so you want to really get in deep. Oil melts—literally melts—all the makeup off your skin." Lim's expert tip? Don't use oil cleansers on an already-wet face. Massage it in to a dry face to melt off all your makeup, then rinse it off with water.

    "The second part of cleansing is the actual cleanser," she says, reaching for an exfoliating formula from Tata Harper. "It has a very, very just like light scrub that will really get into your pores and clean it all out." Lim taps her face dry before she begins. "The reason I dry my face before I apply this is because it almost dries like a clay mask. It's really good if you have dry skin," she says. After smoothing on the cleanser, she layers on the Tata Harper exfoliating mask on top for an extra boost of skin renewal. "I do this about once a week," she says.

    The next step is the lotion P50V from Biologique Recherche. After patting that on, and if she's feeling ambitious, Lim will do a second mask. Her current favorite is the cult-favorite SK-II sheet mask. "It's so cooling. It feels like someone just pouring ice on my face," she says. "This might sound crazy, but what I love about this sheet mask besides the fact that its so hydrating and moisturizing is that it fits my face." After 15 minutes of Instagram and some light cleaning around the house, she removes the mask but not before slathering the extra juice from the bottom of the package all over her neck and chest.
