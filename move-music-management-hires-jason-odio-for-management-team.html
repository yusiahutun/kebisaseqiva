    Move Music Management Hires Jason Odio For Management Team

    13 May 2024, 00:00

    MIAMI (CelebrityAccess)—Move Music Management has hired Jason Odio to help with its expanding roster of artists, which includes Tiago PZK, BHAVI, K4OS and Ambik. Odio will be based in the company’s Miami office.

    A highly regarded executive in the nightclub and hospitality space for over two decades, Odio’s responsibilities ranged from overseeing promotions, team management, and production to booking, operations, and marketing.

    Move Music Management Chief Executive Officer (CEO) Phil Rodriguez said, “Jason brings a wealth of knowledge, experience, and contacts from across the whole creative spectrum—music, fashion, and the arts. As our roster grows, it’s a blessing to have Jason join our Move Music Management team and help grow the careers of our artists.”

    Odio said, “I’m excited to begin this new journey with Move, eager to apply the skills and experience I’ve honed over the years in hospitality to my love for music.”

    Odio, a native of Miami, gained extensive experience in the entertainment industry through his tenure with the renowned Opium Group, where he played various roles for over a decade. During this time, he was instrumental in launching several notable nightclubs, including Mokai, Set, and Mansion. In 2014, Odio embarked on his journey and introduced Sidebar to the Brickell neighborhood in Miami. After an impressive five-year run and earning recognition as the Best New Bar in Miami in 2015, Sidebar transformed Odio’s vision. The venue was reimagined, renovated, and relaunched as La Otra, a vibrant Latin cabaret.

    In addition to his achievements in the nightlife scene, Odio has expanded his portfolio by successfully establishing three restaurants. Bebito’s Café, located in Miami Beach, is his latest culinary venture.
