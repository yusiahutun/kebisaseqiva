    For all those who constantly strive to achieve a flawless golden glow, dark chocolate-like skin, and the perfect sun-kissed look, look what we’ve got for you — the best tan accelerators. So, allow us to make all your tan-related concerns a thing of the past with some top-notch tan accelerators. But before kickstarting your shopping spree, make yourself acquainted with what exactly a tan accelerator is and how a tanning accelerator works. 

    Tanning accelerators, also known as tan intensifiers stimulate the production of melanin which leave behind a dark color payoff. Skin cells are present at the outermost layer of your skin. They are responsible for the production of a pigment known as melanin. And, a good tan enhancer claims to accelerate the natural tanning process (1) and help you achieve a caramel-like skin tone quickly. Tanners contain skin-nourishing ingredients such as walnut oil, carrot oil, olive, oil, cocoa butter, green tea, extract, jojoba extracts, and caramel, to keep your skin smooth, soft, and supple. In addition, they are also infused with dihydroxyacetone (DHA). It is a plant-derived sugar molecule. It chemically reacts with the amino acids in the topmost layer of your skin and produces the necessary pigment once you apply it to the skin (2).

    With a good-quality tan intensifier in your hands, you need not spend hours under the sun and fall prey to sunburn or sun damage. So, ahead is a list of some really good tan enhancers that you can invest in without burning a hole in your pockets. Stay in tune as we also got you some easy-breezy tips at the end to know how to use a tanning accelerator in the right manner.

    How to Choose the Best Tan Accelerators?

    Here are a few aspects that you should take into account before bringing home the best tanning accelerator.

    Skin Type And Concerns: Always analyze your skin type and skin concerns before investing in any tanning enhancer. Do not invest in a tanner that is not meant for your skin type. This might worsen your prevailing skin concerns like rashes, acne, irritation, etc. For those possessing sensitive skin, it is advisable to use a tan accelerator that is gentle and contains no harsh chemicals. Also, note that a tanner shows quick results on a fair skin tone as compared to those with dark or warm tones.

    Ingredients: Make sure your tanner is infused with skin-nourishing ingredients like aloe vera, jojoba extract, shea butter, cocoa butter, green tea extract, coconut, olive, almond, and carrot oils. These ingredients help to calm your skin down while moisturizing it thoroughly. They also aid in minimizing the chances of sun damage or burns. 

    Formula: Ensure that the tan accelerator you are planning to purchase is free from paraben, sulfates, phthalates, or harsh chemicals. Pick a non-comedogenic, dermatologically-tested, allergy-tested, and hypoallergenic tanner to achieve the desired pigment. 

    Texture: Tan accelerators come in a wide range of textures. Some are in the form of lotions, gels, mousses, sprays, and wipes whereas some have a cream or powder-based texture (2). 

    After noting down the above factors, we are sure you must be wondering about the appropriate manner of using the best tan accelerator for desirable results.

    How to Use the Best Tan Accelerators?

    Following are some tips for using the tan accelerator in the right way.

    Tip 1: Use sunscreen before or after applying a tan accelerator. Sunscreen helps in preventing sunburn and damage caused by UVA and UVB rays. According to Dr. Anupama Bisaria, a dermatologist, sunscreen does not prevent tanning completely but they prevent sunspots and cut down the possibility of the signs of aging cropping up. 

    Tip 2: Before applying a tan enhancer containing DHA, it is advisable to shave, wax, or shower. In addition, exfoliating the skin prior to the application of the tanner helps in producing a more even tan (2).

    Tip 3: Avoid moisturizing the area where you have applied the tanner. Moisturizing may interfere with the even absorption of the DHA-infused tanner (2).

    Tip 4: Applying the tanners to the skin in a circular motion helps in preventing a streaky look (2).

    Tip 5: Make sure you do not keep the tan accelerator on your skin for too long if you are a new user. Use it at a slower pace and then gradually increase the time frame.  

    Tip 6: If you haven’t tried using a tan enhancer before, then make sure that you do a patch test.

    FAQ's

    How long should you leave the tan accelerator on?

    You should leave the tan accelerator for at least 30 minutes before heading out in the sun.

    What makes a good tan accelerator?

    SPF, antioxidants, vitamins, essential fatty acids, and other melanin-producing agents make a good tan accelerator.

    Can you wash the tan accelerator off?

    Yes. You can wash the tan accelerator off after 2 to 4 hours. Washing it away immediately won’t give you desirable results.

    Do you put the tan accelerator first or sunscreen?

    You can apply the tan accelerator before as well as after sunscreen. If you apply sunscreen after the tan accelerator, then it will slow down the tanning process whereas when you apply it before the tan accelerator, then it will help in preventing sunburns.
