    Local Food Delivery And Pickup Available During COVID-19

    In light of COVID-19, GovernorRon DeSantis has ordered all Florida restaurants to close except for delivery and takeout options.Extraprotocols are also being implemented, such as frequent and thorough hand-washing,cleaning and disinfecting procedures, as well asmonitoring the health of employees.

    04 Jul 2024, 00:00

    In light of COVID-19, Governor Ron DeSantis has ordered all Florida restaurants to close except for delivery and takeout options. Extra protocols are also being implemented, such as frequent and thorough hand-washing, cleaning and disinfecting procedures, as well as monitoring the health of employees.

    We’ve compiled restaurants in South Florida offering delivery, takeout and curbside pickup for people to support during this difficult time. Remember to be mindful of social distancing, and take care of your physical and mental health.

    Martin County

    Dining at a Distance has compiled a master list of all local restaurants offering to-go, pickup and delivery options on the Treasure Coast during the COVID-19 pandemic. For the complete list, visit diningatadistance.com/martin-county.

    • Deliteful Kitchen: Pickup is available from 1 p.m. to 3 p.m. daily. Adult meals are $8, and kids meals are $6. The local store is selling keto granola, microgreens, hand sanitizer, banana bread, honey, pasta, juice, tea and produce, as well as trays of vegan lasagna for $30. For pickup, call 772-634-3901.
    • Kyle G’s is offering a reduced price menu, alongside $6 individual meals and $25 family-style meals at the Martin County Fair Grounds. The restaurant is also working with community partners in Martin and St. Lucie counties to offer free meals to unemployed individuals and seniors with little to no income, also located at the Martin County Fairgrounds. If you are in need, please register with one of the following organizations so your needs and dietary restrictions can be met:
      • Elevate Hope, Port Salerno; 772-291-2665
      • Gertrude Walden Childcare, Stuart; 772-283-6321
      • GraceWay Village,  St Lucie; 772-448-4903
      • In the image of Christ, Fort Pierce; 772-461-7788

        House of Hope – Golden Gate; 772-286-4673

      • Mustard Seed Ministries, Fort Pierce; 772-465-6021
      • Dunbar Early Learning, Hobe Sound; 772-545-0808
    • Bagel City South: Pickup is available from 7 a.m. to 1 p.m.; 561-393-5383.
    • Casa D’Angelo: Takeout and delivery available only, and delivery is free through the restaurant. Get 25% off the entire wine list on curbside pickup and delivery orders. App service with Uber Eats, Door Dash and Delivery Dudes to come.
    • Coolinary Cafe: The Palm Beach Gardens eatery is debuting its new “Pig in a Pickle” sandwich shop pop up today, March 20. “Pig in a Pickle” is available for curbside takeout Monday through Saturday from 11 a.m. to 8 p.m, and dine-in is suspended; 561-249-6760.
    • Deck 84, Burt & Max’s: Both restaurants are open for takeout, curbside pickup and delivery.
    • Max’s Grille: Delivery and takeout only from 11 a.m. to 8 p.m. daily.
    • Prezzo: Delivery and takeout only, Monday through Friday from 11 a.m. to 9 p.m., and Saturday and Sunday 4 p.m. to 9 p.m.
    • Saiko-i Sushi Lounge: With every $100 gift card purchased, receive a $20 gift card. Takeout orders are 10 percent off with delivery available through Delivery Dudes and Uber Eats; 561-544-0087.
    • Yakitori Sake House: Every takeout order is 10 percent off, and delivery is available through Delivery Dudes; 561-393-5888.
    • U-Tiki Beach: Available for takeout from 4:30 p.m. to 8 p.m.; 561-406-2210.
    • Alma Grill at Gulfstream Park Village: Call 786-899-7999 for Shabbat catering from 11 a.m.  to 11 p.m. daily.
    • American Social Las Olas: Call  954-764-7005 to order in-store pickup, or receive delivery through UberEats. Diners will receive a free drink card with every $15 spent.
    • Casa D’Angelo: Takeout and delivery available only, and delivery is free through the restaurant. Get 25% off the entire wine list on curbside pickup and delivery orders. App service with Uber Eats, Door Dash and Delivery Dudes to come.
    • Christine Lee’s at Gulfstream Park Village: Takeout and delivery are available nightly from 4 p.m. to 9 p.m. by calling 954-457-6255.
    • DUNE: A takeaway menu is available for diners to enjoy, and hours have been extended for takeout services, from 11 a.m. to 8 p.m. daily. Only credit cards are accepted; 754-900-4059.
    • Legends Tavern and Grille: Curbside pickup is available at all four locations (Sunrise, Deerfield Beach, Lighthouse Point and Pompano). With the exception of Pompano, all locations are offering delivery services through UberEats and Delivery Dudes (except Sunrise). All takeout and online orders will receive a $10 off gift card for future use. Takeout is available from 11:30 a.m. to 9 p.m.; Sunrise: 954-742-7115; Deerfield Beach: 754-212-2655; Lighthouse Point: 754-229-8932; Pompano: 954-933-3859
    • LIME: Delivery, curbside pickup and takeout orders are $5 off when ordered through the LIME website using code #5OFF. In-house dining is suspended until further notice, but customers can add any salsa bar items to their order.
    • Louie Bossi’s: Curbside pickup and delivery are both available. Place your order and pay over the phone by calling 954-356-6699.
    • Metro Diner: Dining options are available for online pickup, delivery on DoorDash and UberEats, and curbside pickup. Metro Meal Packs are also available to feed families of four to six people, including family-sized breakfast, lunch and dinner entrees; breakfast sandwich packs, family combo platters, sandwich packs; and fresh salads.
    • Phat Boy Sushi: Takeout and delivery are available daily from 11:30 a.m. to 10 p.m. Call 954-990-8669 or order online.
    • Rivertail Fort Lauderdale: Pickup and local delivery are available from noon to 8 p.m. All phone orders are 10% off with no delivery fee, call 954-306-2665.
    • Shooters Waterfront: Diners get $10 meals with curbside pickup and delivery from 11 a.m. to 8 p.m. daily. To-go family meal packages that serve four to six people are also available.
    • Steelpan: Takeout is available from 8 a.m. to 8 p.m. with limited breakfast.
    • TacoCraft: To-go and curbside pickup are available at the Lauderdale-by-the-Sea location. Free delivery is available through UberEats.
    • Vale Food Co.: Place an order by calling ahead at 954-306-8692, or order online for pickup. UberEats is also offering delivery.
    • Yard House at Gulfstream Park Village: Takeout is available through online ordering 11 a.m. to 9 p.m., and delivery through Gubhub is available daily until 10 p.m.; 954-454-9950.
    • Drunken Dragon: Dine-in is suspended. Takeout and delivery are available daily from 1 p.m. to 10 p.m. using UberEats, Grubhub, DoorDash and Postmates; 305-397-8556. For UberEats, spend $30 and save $8; for GrubHub, get $5 off any $25 order; for Postmates, get $5 off any $20 order; and new customers on DoorDash receive free delivery on orders more than $15.
    • Flanigan’s: Curbside pickup and delivery are currently available at the following Flanigan’s locations: Coconut Grove, North Miami, Kendall, Hialeah, Fort Lauderdale, Doral, Surfside, Pinecrest, Westchester and Lake Worth.
    • Pink Taco: Takeout is available, and online orders have free delivery, which can be placed from 11 a.m. to 8 p.m. The full dinner menu is also available; 305-507-8980.
