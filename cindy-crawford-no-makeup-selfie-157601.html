    She’s a natural beauty! While Cindy Crawford is often required to rock a full face of beauty products for her job, her latest Instagram post proves she could model without a drop of makeup. The supermodel took to the social media platform on Thursday, April 5 to share a stunning no makeup selfie, and at age 52, there isn’t a wrinkle in sight on Cindy’s gorgeous face. “Puppy pillow,” the mom-of-two captioned the adorable photo, in which she lounged on the floor with her dog Widget. Check out the video above to see the beauty’s makeup-free face!

    Last summer, Cindy participated in Vogue‘s 73 Questions, answering a plethora of interesting questions thrown at her on the spot. During the fun video, she even revealed her simple secret to aging gracefully. “Be happy,” the stunner explained. But there has to be more to her beauty regimen, since she looks like she hasn’t aged a day. Luckily, she did divulge a few more of her beauty secrets. Her must-have products are sunscreen, lip gloss, and mascara, and her nighttime beauty routine involves washing her face and applying beauty serum and night cream. We’re taking notes!

    Cindy Crawford in 1990.

    One person who seems to have inherited all of Cindy’s beauty genes is her 16-year-old daughter, Kaia Gerber. In fact, the mother-daughter duo could be twins separated by 36 years — they look that much alike! “When I look at my mom, aside from physical features (of course), we share many of the same mannerisms,” the budding model explained in a Teen Vogue interview conducted along with Cindy. “I notice it when looking back on past videos of her. I don’t know if it’s genetic, or something I’ve just caught onto, but our actions mimic one another.”

    The proud mom added, “You’re so much more confident than I was! I didn’t start modeling until I was nearly 17. You have a sense of what feels right for you. I don’t worry about you staying true to yourself.” Kaia certainly has the best teacher and role model to look up to in the industry!
