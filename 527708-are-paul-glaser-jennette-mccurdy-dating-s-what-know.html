    Who are Paul Glaser and Jennette McCurdy?

    Paul Michael Glaser is an American actor and director. He is best known for his role as Detective Dave Starsky on the 1970s television series Starsky & Hutch. He was born on March 25th, 1943, in Cambridge, Massachusetts, United States.

    On the other hand, Jennette McCurdy is an American actress, singer, and writer. She rose to prominence with her role as Sam Puckett in the Nickelodeon sitcom iCarly.
