    Johnny Depp Signs With Global Artist Management

    LOS ANGELES (CelebrityAccess) – Johnny Depp has signed with Global Artist Management. GAM’s Paul Geary and Steve Wood will manage all aspects of Depp’s career except for film, which continues to be represented by CAA.

    Geary and Wood launched GAM last year, and its roster currently includes Godsmack, Aerosmith guitarist Joe Perry and Hoobastank. Depp, Perry and Alice Cooper’s supergroup Hollywood Vampires is also co-managed by GAM and Shep Gordon.

    As an actor, Depp has been nominated for three Oscars and 10 Golden Globes, winning one of the latter for his lead performance in 2007’s Sweeney Todd.
