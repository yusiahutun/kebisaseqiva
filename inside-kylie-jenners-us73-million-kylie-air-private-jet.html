    Let’s take a closer look …

    Mind-blowing catering on board

    Making travelling first-class look second-rate is the unbelievably decadent seven-course menu on board the make-up mogul’s private jet named Kylie Air. (Are we sending a trend here?)

    Momager Kris Jenner shared the seven-course menu on board Kylie Air that listed mouth-watering delicacies like roasted tomato bisque, lentil soup and spinach ravioli with butter sage pesto.

    Kylie Air also offers Kardashian clan favourites like Chinese chicken salad, seared Mahimahi (dolphinfish) with pineapple mango salsa, sautéed spinach, grilled vegetables and pomme purée.

    A plethora of luxurious amenities

    The benefits of being a billionairess is that your private jet is likely to be equipped with other luxurious amenities like an entertainment room, master suite, giant wardrobe and even pink mood lighting.

    Kylie Air prioritises comfort by including plush white seating, pink blankets, custom KylieSkin pillows and cosy sleepers for long flights.

    It’s all personalised too

    There are enough reminders that the stunning aircraft belongs to Kylie Jenner, such as personalised details on coasters, decor and “KylieSkin” emblazoned across the side of the plane.
