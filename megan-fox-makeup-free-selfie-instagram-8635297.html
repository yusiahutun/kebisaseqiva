    Megan Fox is known for her over-the-top glam. Whether she's sporting statement red lips with matching cherry-colored hair, or a manicure made out of 14-carat white gold, she always goes full tilt when it's time to get ready. But on Wednesday, the actress decided to go back to basics and offered fans a rare glimpse at her natural beauty. 

    In a makeup-free selfie shared to Instagram, Fox captured a candid bare-faced moment following her weekend spent at Coachella. "repairing my hair (and 26 inches of extensions) post coachella," she wrote alongside a photo of herself with waist-length blue waves and her Wella Professionals hair products lined up on the bathroom counter next to her. 

    Like her glam, Fox's outfit was also uncharacteristically low-key. Wearing nothing but a black bra and pair of red-and-black flannel boxers, she showed off her toned midsection and collection of tattoos â€” including her latest arm sleeve mural. 

    Over the weekend, Fox debuted her new bright blue hair extensions with a pantless look at Coachella. For the occasion, she wore an oversized black leather Acne jacket with a long Nirvana T-shirt and accessorized with a cowboy hat from 8 Other Reasons and thigh-high boots. As for her new hairstyle, Fox explained to People, "I actually have a blue bob right now. We added the extensions in to give it more Coachella energy. I think I bleached it, and I destroyed it. So I might as well run through all the colors before I go brown."
