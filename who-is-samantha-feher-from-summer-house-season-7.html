    Summer House, Bravo’s famous reality TV program, is scheduled to air season 7 in less than a day. Aside from the returning cast members, the series will feature new characters who will join the rest of the celebrities in the Hamptons. Season 7 of Summer House will debut on Bravo on Monday, February 13, 2023, at 10 p.m. ET/ 9 p.m. CT. Viewers who miss the show may watch it the following day on Peacock. Individuals with appropriate login credentials and a subscription may also watch episodes live on YouTube TV. Summer House was initially renewed for season 7 in May 2022. Samantha Feher is one of the cast members that will be entering the house this season. She is one of the newest cast members and works as a writer and entrepreneur.

    Samantha, who is from New Jersey, had always wanted to live in New York. With just a few weeks before the debut of the forthcoming season of Summer House, here’s everything you need to know about the show’s newest addition, Samantha Feher.

    Samantha Feher, a Summer House season 7 cast member, created the Feher agency in December 2021.

    Samantha Feher is a writer who lives by the mantra “work hard, play hard.”

    According to her Bravo bio:

    “Samantha, who is extremely single, has a steady roster of four or five guys and is keeping things casual until she meets someone worth dumping everything for. But, with her extremely ambitious nature and party animal lifestyle, will she be able to find a spouse who can keep up?”

    According to her LinkedIn profile, the Manhattan-based celebrity started her career at Cosmopolitan. She then served as Cynthia Rowley’s head of socials. Samantha then began her career as an entrepreneur, and she is the founder and CEO of Feher agency, which was launched in December 2021. Feher Agency is a creative marketing business that strives to transform a person’s social footprint into a social fingerprint. She is also the creator and CEO of Summer Club, formerly known as the Brunch Club online shop.

    Her entrepreneurial experience is described in her LinkedIn bio:

    “In 2021, she started Feher Firm, an eponymous creative agency that assists both early-stage companies and well-established businesses in fashion, tech, publishing, and travel in building healthy social identities and communities.”

    It goes on to say:

    “Samantha is also the founder and CEO of Supper Club, a digital retail firm, and in her spare time, she provides career counseling and professional development services to ambitious young women throughout the United States.”

    Samantha is active on social media, with over 52k Instagram followers. She often updates about her trips and also shares photos and videos with the other cast members of the reality program, as well as her friends and family. She also shows off her collection of creative and out-of-the-box styles that captivate her internet devotees.
