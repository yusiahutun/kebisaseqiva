    During quarantine, my daily wardrobe quickly transitioned from jeans, sweaters, and dresses to matching sweatpants and sweatshirts. That shift also included ditching my uncomfortable underwire bras for wire-free ones. While I've started incorporating pants with buttons and zippers back into my wardrobe, I've officially adopted the wire-free lifestyle. And I'm especially obsessed with the ultra comfortable True & Co True Body V-Neck Bra.

    As someone who hates bras, it's not often that I find one I can tolerate wearing all day, but this True & Co style is exactly that. It's made from a super soft and stretchy blend of nylon and elastane. It has a V-neckline in both the front and back, and it goes on over your head without a painful clasp in the back digging into your skin. Plus, the bra has removable pads, so you can customize the amount of support it provides.

Since it's available in so many colors and doesn't even look like a bra, I often wear it as a bralette that peaks through my clothes.
