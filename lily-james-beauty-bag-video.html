    Lily James has captured hearts as Cinderella, Downton Abbey’s Lady Rose, Natasha in War and Peace, Mrs de Winter in Rebecca, and Pamela Anderson in Pam & Tommy – and throughout her screen career she’s taken on various beauty guises, from that of filmic English rose to Hollywood bombshell.

    But it's not after a stint in the make-up chair that she feels her best, though. “I feel most beautiful after a long night’s sleep,” the actress reveals in our exclusive video, above. “With my friends, after a bath… [it’s] all those things that are good for you” that make her radiate.

    Sleep, James explains – dressed in her Olivia von Halle pyjamas – is something she’s preoccupied with. “I used to be an amazing sleeper but it’s getting worse, and I’m scared – because my mum’s an insomniac.” Two items saving her on this front are the Artah Sleep Tonic (a botanical supplement) and her Slip Contour Sleep Mask. “I love this, it’s so comfortable… I now sleep on their pillows as well,” she says sharing a tip about the “wrinkle-preventative” properties of silk.

    These are just two of her cherished possessions housed in her beauty bag – the Anya Hindmarch Lotions and Potions Pouch in Hot Pink, no less – that’s “full to the brim and bursting”. In the show-and-tell, above, James shares many more wonderful finds.

    For her hair, which “gets super frizzy and I’ve dyed it way too many times,” she swears by Tangle Teezer’s The Wet Detangler and the “amazing” Olaplex Nº.7 Bonding Oil (“it makes your hair feel soft and look finished”). For her skin, she recommends the Charlotte Tilbury Charlotte’s Magic Cream which she is “never without”, and the Jillian Dempsey Gold Sculpting Bar which she likes to follow up moisturising with. “Gemma Chan coined them eye-brators,” she laughs, explaining that the vibrating facial tool “gets the blood flowing and is kind of like my meditation”.

    Given her role as a Charlotte Tilbury ambassador, James is unsurprisingly enamoured with many of the make-up artist brand’s products. The Beautiful Skin Radiant Concealer and Hollywood Glow Glide Face Architect Highlighter are behind her even, luminous complexion, she demonstrates, while also revealing her signature lip colour is the cult Matte Revolution Lipstick in Pillow Talk Original. “I wear this almost every day,” she says, showing the bullet which she's proudly used down to a mere stub.

    Her elegant beauty looks now are a far cry from her early ones, she recalls. “In my teenage years I think I only embraced horrific beauty mistakes! I concealed my lips, I wore too much fake tan, I plucked my eyebrows to within an inch of their life – I’m so grateful they grew back!”

    Agata Pospieszynska

    Now, beauty for James is very ritualistic. “I cleanse, tone, moisturise every day and night,” she says. “And I love, love long baths – with as much bath salts and bubbles and oils as possible.” Scent is something James has a deep connection with, too. “Fragrance is something I use a lot for building characters,” she says, noting how it evokes such strong memories. “For different characters I use a different fragrance – so I put it on in the morning and it's part of getting into character. And it’s nice now when I go back and smell them; I’m right back in that time.”

    To hear more about James’ personal and professional journey with beauty – and the products she swears by, watch her ‘Inside my beauty bag’ video above.
