    Margaret Kenyatta All Smiles as Young Pupils Recite Beautiful Poem in Her Honour: &quot;First Lady Ma'am&quot;

    Outgoing First Lady Margaret Kenyatta has been thrilled by young school-going kids who recited a poem in her honour. The presentation happened during the farewell ceremony of the fourth commander-in-chief, President Uhuru Kenyatta, by the military.

    02 Feb 2024, 00:00
    • KDF today honoured the outgoing president Uhuru Kenyatta with gifts, just a few days before he hands over power to William Ruto
    • Pupils from Moi Airbase Primary School stole the show with their poem serenading the outgoing first lady Margaret Kenyatta
    • They thanked her for her initiatives like the Beyond Zero campaign and mobile clinics and said thanks to such programmes, kids can now celebrate their fifth birthdays

    Outgoing First Lady Margaret Kenyatta has been thrilled by young school-going kids who recited a poem in her honour.

    Students serenade Margaret Kenyatta

    The presentation happened during the farewell ceremony of the fourth commander-in-chief, President Uhuru Kenyatta, by the military.

    The Moi Airbase Primary School students began by saluting Uhuru and showering him with praises, referring to him as the excellency.

    Uhuru, being so excited, clapped as he laughed when the young pupils finished their first stanza.

    "We stand here, Mr President, to thank you for giving us a treasure to freely. A great social activist," they continued.

    They also left the outgoing president with teary eyes after amusing him with the part of the poem celebrating Margaret for walking and supporting him in his tenure.

    "Our first lady mama Margaret. We salute you, ma'am. The beyond zero initiative is now every mother's smile, the mobile clinics are a joy to every family," they recited.

    They reminded her that every child can now celebrate their fifth birthday thanks to her initiatives, and they thanked her for giving Kenya hope.

    The outgoing first lady who was standing all through the poem, dressed in a blue coat and black dress, couldn't hide her joy as she smiled and laughed each time the kids called her name.

    Uhuru kisses Margaret after winning city marathon

    In a separate story, Uhuru could not conceal his pride and joy after Margaret emerged as the winner in the 5km race.

    While awarding her, the head of state warmly kissed his wife on the cheeks while also hugging her and holding her hands.

    The president and the first lady flagged off the annual Nairobi City Marathon on the morning of Sunday, May 8 2022.
