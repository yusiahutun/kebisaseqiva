    Who is Steve Allen?

    Steve Allen is an English radio presenter who hosted the early morning breakfast show on LBC until 2023. Fans grew fond of him over the years. Steve is notably known for his extensive broadcasting career spanning over four decades. He was born on March 17, 1954, and will be 69 in 2023.

    Interestingly, Allen’s first job wasn’t in radio but as a nightclub disc jockey. While working as a disc jockey, he met his long-time friend Dale Winton and joined the United Biscuits Network, a radio station broadcasting in the United Biscuits factories.

    Allen’s journey with LBC started in 1979 after UBN closed. He joined the company as a presenter of the Night Extra programme. Allen then moved to The Night is Young. Steve has worked in different roles in the company over the years, including being a presenter on the afternoon show for London Talkback Radio and Saturday Night Out.
