    Kelly Clarkson wore two bold sports-themed looks to host the 12th annual NFL Honors in Phoenix ahead of Sunday's Super Bowl LVII.

    Last night, the "Stronger" singer arrived at the event—held at the city's Symphony Hall—wearing a dramatic black velvet Adidas x Gucci gown with long sleeves and an even longer train that flowed onto the red carpet. The piece featured the activewear brand's iconic three stripes, in white, down the sleeves. The star accessorized with mirrored chandelier earrings and a couple silver rings. As for glam, she kept things classic, opting for a sleek, straight hairdo; a smoky eye; and a rosy, glossy lip.

    Anthony Behar - PA Images//Getty Images

    Later in the evening, Clarkson got into the Super Bowl spirit in a vibrant blue-and-white Dallas Cowboys jersey-inspired gown. She took to the stage in the sporty dress, which had a silver 88 emblazoned on the bodice (in honor of former Cowboys wide receiver Drew Pearson), as well as the last names of four other team players, including running back Ezekiel Elliott and tight end Dalton Schultz.

    Cooper Neill//Getty Images

    Clarkson is the first female to host the awards show. Past hosts include Keegan-Michael Key, Steve Harvey, and Alec Baldwin.

    Super Bowl LVII will take place Sunday, February 12, at State Farm Stadium in Glendale, Arizona. The teams competing are the Philadelphia Eagles and the Kansas City Chiefs.

    And equally, if not more importantly, Rihanna will make her highly anticipated musical comeback at halftime with a big performance.
