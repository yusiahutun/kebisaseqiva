    Most customers in the current market buy goods because of the brand behind them. Branding allows you to build relationships with your audience and turn them into loyal customers. The same applies to excavating businesses where the best brands attract loyal customers. So, what brands of excavators do you know?

    Excavating business involves creating a construction project site. Excavators establish the foundation for the project by turning a barren field or an existing structure into a busy construction site. Machines used in this business are equally heavy because they require much expertise.

    What are the best brands of excavators in the world?

    There are many reputable excavating companies in the world. These companies belong to a broad range of sectors because each business has a distinct area of expertise. Below are some of thebest brands in this field.

    1. Caterpillar Inc. (CAT)

    The most significant excavator manufacturer in the world is Caterpillar excavator (CAT). Caterpillar has more than 300 machines in its product line, including numerous different-sized excavators. It has a massive range of excavators that may suit you, regardless of whether your activity involves landscaping, building, or agriculture.

    CAT is one of the old excavator brands in the construction industry. It delivers top service performance, and its equipment is durable with excellent fuel efficiency. Caterpillar is a US-based company with its headquarters in Illinois.

    2. Volvo CE

    Volvo is one of the world's most popular excavator companies. Volvo is based in Sweden and deals specifically with equipment for construction-related industries. The company uses advanced experience to advise customers on the best machine in every application.

    Volvo excavators are designed with advanced hydraulics. The company sells a variety of heavy machinery, including excavators, wheel loaders, articulated dump trucks, and compactors. They also deliver long-lasting performance, great power, and versatile control.

    3. Komatsu

    Komatsu is a global corporation with a focus on mining and construction machinery. The company's headquarters are in Minato, Tokyo, Japan, and it is the second-largest construction machinery producer after Caterpillar.

    The company has a pretty impressive excavator range from mini excavators to the more powerful large mining excavators. Additionally, their excavators are technologically advanced, with some having built-in 3D GPS systems.

    4. Doosan

    Doosan is a Korean multinational company primarily manufacturing and supplying heavy construction equipment. Doosan has been one of the top 10 equipment producers in the world since 2018. Doosan produces some of the best excavators in the industry, such as mini excavators, crawler excavators, and wheel excavators.

    Doosan offers a brand-new building environment that uses cutting-edge ICT technologies for remote control. Additionally, the company provides reliable long-flight solutions with fuel cell technology, leading the growth of the unmanned aerial vehicle (UAV) industry.

    5. Hitachi

    Hitachi is one of the best excavator brands in the world based in Japan. The company specializes in producing, selling, and servicing a diverse construction and transportation equipment range. These excavators are made to function at the highest level while maintaining the lowest possible overhead costs. They are also versatile and fuel-efficient.

    6. JCB (Joseph Cyril Bamford Excavators Ltd)

    JCB is among the most common excavator brands based in Britain. Joseph Cyril Bamford founded JCB in October 1945. The company has evolved over the years and produces advanced engineering equipment. JCB produces, services and sells a range of excavators from a weight of 1.8-ton 18Z-1 to the 10-ton 100C-1.

    7. Liebherr Group

    Liebherr Group is a Europe-based company that produces and supplies yellow excavator brands. The famous company is known to have created the most powerful mobile crane- Liebherr's LTM 11200-9. Liebherr Group also boasts of producing one of the most extended telescopic boom lifts that spread to almost 100 meters.

    8. Hyundai Heavy Industries (HHI)

    The late Chung Ju-Yung founded Hyundai Heavy Industries in 1972. The company is known to be the world's best shipbuilding powerhouse globally. Hyundai produces an extensive range of excavators with excellent features, including precision control, hydraulic power, and great speed.

    HHI has been actively gaining ground in the markets for construction equipment worldwide. The company has five overseas production facilities in China, India, and Brazil. Their product line includes 1.5-ton excavators, 30-ton forklifts, and Korea's largest 120-ton excavators.

    9. CASE excavators

    The CASE is a brand of heavy construction equipment renowned for power, durability and flexibility. The company produces a wide range of construction equipment globally. CASE is responsible for manufacturing the largest known excavator, the CASE CX750D. CASE is one of the old excavator brands in the world with the most efficient equipment.

    The company has upgraded such that the next-generation E-Series raises the efficiency standard with six essential energy control characteristics. This was achieved by combining the D-Series' tried-and-true energy-saving features with new work modes and hydraulic settings.

    10. Kobelco

    Kobelco excavators are a series of blue excavator brands from Japan. They are known to be among the best excavators with unbeatable value in the market. The company offers SR series, speciality, and conventional excavators.

    Kobelco is known to have over 90 years of history and technical expertise. Therefore, doing business with them guarantees an outstanding warranty in the market and a reliable network.

    11. John Deere & Company

    This US-based company deals with construction, forestry machinery, technology goods, and services. The company operates under the most common excavator, the John Deere brand.

    The company's slogan is "Nothing Runs Like a Deere", with a logo of a leaping deer. The leaping deer symbolizes the brand's never-ending pursuit of growth and wealth. Deere & Company machinery includes Super Heavy-Duty Tractors, E series, and D series.

    12. Terex

    Terex is a well-known brand for reliable compact excavators. Their excavators are cost-effective because they are smaller, easy to maintain, and easy to operate. Terex is focused on developing affordable, quality equipment without compromising features and quality.

    13. Longking

    Longking excavators is a company that deals with a series of yellow excavator brands. The company was initially known as China Infrastructure Machinery Holdings Limited. Longkings deals with distributing road rollers, excavators, wheel loaders, and forklifts.

    14. Takeuchi

    Takeuchi is one of the old excavator brands in Japan. The company started by manufacturing environmental equipment. Takeuchi excavators have over ten different models of compact excavators. The excavators have various valuable attachments, the most popular being the 5.7-tone Takeuchi TB260.

    15. Bobcat

    Bobcat Company is the industry leader in designing, producing, marketing, and distributing small machinery. The company was established in 1947 in the United States of America. Compact excavators from Bobcat are renowned for being adaptable and capable of performing various jobs.

    Who is the largest manufacturer of excavators?

    Caterpillar Inc. is the largest manufacturer of excavators and is among the best brands of excavators in the world. Other top excavator brands include Volvo CE, Bobcat, Longking, and Komatsu.
