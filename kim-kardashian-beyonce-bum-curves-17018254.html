    Body positive warrior Nicole Herring has amassed an army of 160,000 fans thanks to her killer curves and peachy behind.

    It comes as reality star Kim's social media fan base nosedived by 100,000 followers after unfiltered photos of her famous bum went viral.

    Nicole's bottom has seen her getting up to 20,000 likes per post.

    And it's all natural.

    Revealing her journey on her Instagram account, Nicole wrote: "I have always been bullied growing up.

    "I had wonky teeth, bad skin and was the ‘fat’ one of the group.

    "Hitting college is when I started the gym, I only intended to lose fat because of the people I was surrounded by and because that was the pressure of society, then I found my passion for weightlifting and ever since then I’ve been bettering myself and my body."

    The brunette hits the gym and lifts weights to achieve her sensational physique.

    She defied cruel bullies to become confident in her own skin – before sharing her progress online in a bid to inspire thousands of people.

    "Gaining muscle, losing fat, gaining fat is all part of my journey, I know what works for me and I love that I still have so much to work for.

    "I have so much more confidence now. Looking back at myself, I had no confidence, I used to wear baggy clothes to hide my fat and big thighs."

    And Nicole told how developing a "thick skin" has helped her to laugh off comments from vile haters.

    "I just use it as part of my motivation to keep making an impact," she added.

    Nicole has posed in a series of skin-tight dresses and revealing thongs to proudly show off her figure.

    The pictures have won the approval of her fans, who described her as "simply flawless".

    Others posted how her pictures were "beautiful".

    And one follower told how Nicole's bum "got me losing my mind".
