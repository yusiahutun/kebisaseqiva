    Who is Chris Pine?

    Christopher "Chris" Pine, born in Los Angeles, California, on August 26, 1980, is an American actor. He is famous for his roles in Wonder Woman 1984, Wonder Woman (2017) and Star Trek (2009-2016). His father, Robert Pine, mother, Gwynne Gilford and older sister, Katherine, were also actors.

    Chris Pine's dating history

    Over the years, Chris Pine has dated several models and actresses. Here's a look at his dating history.

    Audrina Patridge

    In 2021, The Hills star Audrina Patridge disclosed a brief romantic involvement with Chris Pine in 2009, sparked by their meeting at the ShoWest Awards. However, their relationship fizzled out due to Pine's discomfort with the paparazzi's attention, which was integral to Patridge's lifestyle.

    Olivia Munn

    In 2010, Chris was rumoured to be in a relationship with Hollywood actress Olivia Munn; their relationship lasted for several months. While the pair never officially confirmed their relationship, they were captured together severally, including on a beautiful dinner date in January 2010.

    Dominique Piek

    From 2011 to 2013, Chris Pine was romantically involved with South African model Dominique Piek. Their relationship became public when they unexpectedly appeared on the red carpet at the 2012 Cannes Film Festival, officially announcing their status as a couple.

    Zoe Kravitz

    The rumours surrounding Chris Pine's relationship with Batwoman actress Zoe Kravitz began in September 2014 after they were spotted together at a Coldplay concert. Months later, they were spotted partying at Tao Nightclub during the Sundance Film Festival.

    They were again photographed arriving together at a pre-Oscar dinner party at Madeo restaurant in Beverly Hills, California. They remained a close friend over the years, and Chris Pine even attended Zoe's and Karl Glusman's wedding in 2019.

    Vail Bloom

    Did Vail Bloom date Chris Pine? In May 2015, Chris was spotted with The Young and Restless actress Vail Bloom. The duo was photographed cuddling during a lunch date in Hollywood, followed by a stroll and shopping. Despite their public outings, neither Pine nor Bloom made any public statements regarding the nature of their relationship at the time.

    Sofia Boutella

    In September 2016, Chris Pine was romantically linked with his Star Trek Beyond co-star Sofia Boutella. Their relationship gained attention when Sofia posted a picture of them together at the Hell or High Water premiere, accompanied by an intriguing caption about Chris.

    Six months later, Pine and Boutella were spotted attending the NYLON Midnight Garden party at Coachella, where reports suggested they displayed flirtatious behaviour towards each other.

    Annabelle Wallis

    Chris Pine's recent relationship was with British actress Annabelle Wallis. Their relationship first made headlines in March 2018 after they were spotted at London's Heathrow Airport. The couple confirmed their romantic relationship that summer and even vacationed on a yacht in Italy.

    While dating, the couple stayed out of the limelight, avoiding red-carpet appearances together. After being together for four years, Annabelle Wallis and Chris Pine split in March 2022.
