    Chloe Bennet was not given the last name “Bennet” at birth, as this is her professional name. The Agent of S.H.I.E.L.D. The actress was born on April 18, 1992, in Chicago, Illinois, as Chloe Wang. Her parents are American, but her father, Bennet Wang, is of Chinese descent, while her mother, Stephanie Crane, is of white descent.

    Bennet grew up in southern Chicago and her home was always full of children of different ethnicities. At the age of twelve, she attended St. Ignatius College Preparatory School and a larger-than-life extroverted Chloe began taking steps towards entertainment and show business in general by joining the Second City Youth Ensemble, hoping to develop her acting and singing talents.

    Chloe Bennet Career

    In 2007, when she was fifteen years old, Chloe Bennet (then Chloe Wang) moved to China on the advice of her then-manager with the aim of exploring the entertainment circuit in Beijing. Her first task during her stay with her grandmother was to learn her mother tongue – Mandarin. She did this in an effort to integrate and gain acceptance in the Chinese market, and it proved to be a wise move.

    She enrolled for courses in Shanghai and soon enough learned enough to sing her first single in Mandarin, entitled “Uh Oh”. The title, which was also released in English and appeared in 2011, was a hit in China. It became very popular and gained traction as it was broadcast by the country’s major media.

    Following the success of the single Uh Oh, in the same year Chloe released a second single called “Every Day in Between”. As her popularity grew, she was given the opportunity to perform at a charity event for the victims of the 2008 Sichuan earthquake, and this was her first live performance. Her performance at the Zebra Music Festival was the start of her career in showbiz, and soon Chloe was being celebrated by all the major advertising agencies in China. She was featured in Chinese Cosmopolitan, Vogue, Elle, In Style, and other fashion magazines in the country.

    In an effort to develop a career on the screens, Chloe Bennet returned to the USA in 2010. She was looking for acting roles through auditions but came across a brick wall. According to her, directors looked over her because of her name – Chloe Wang – and since then she has been speaking openly about her perceived racism in Hollywood. However, Chloe decided to drop the family name “Wang” and adopt her father’s first name “Bennet”, which, she claims, cast a spell when she started taking on acting roles.

    She made her acting debut when she appeared in the dance television series “The Nightlife”, which was broadcast on the TeenNick network in 2010 and also featured Nick Cannon. Her next appearance on the big screen was to come two years later when she played a supporting role as Hailey in the television series Nashville. The following year, 2013, Chloe Bennet was featured in Agents of S.H.I.E.L.D. Her character in the series – Daisy “Skye” Johnson/Quake – is one of the main characters in the series, and so she has remained in the series (which had 5 seasons) to this day.

    Parents

    Chloe Bennet is born into an American family of mixed origins. She is the only daughter of Bennet Wang and Stephanie Lynn Crane. Her father, Bennet, is an investment banker of Chinese descent and her mother, an internist, is of English, German, Irish and Scottish descent. She is the only girl in the family as her six siblings are all boys. Of her six brothers, three have the same parents as her, while the other three were adopted (one of who is Mexican-Philippine and two are African-American).
