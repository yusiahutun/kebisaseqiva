    22 Sep 2024, 00:00

    The America's Got Talent judge dazzled fans in the sparkly outfit

      Sofia Vergara never fails to turn heads when it comes to her gorgeous outfits, and the newly single star looked better than ever in a lace bodysuit and sparkling sequin skirt on Friday.

      The judge shared a series of photos from her night out at Beyoncé Renaissance World Tour.

      Sofia looked gorgeous in a strapless black sheer top which featured a fitted bodice and floral lace detailing, which the actress styled with a dazzling sequin midi skirt. 

      The 51-year-old wore her hair in her signature straight style for the night out, opting for a glam makeup look consisting of a touch of brown eyeshadow, a rosy blush, and a matte nude lip. 

      Sharing several snaps of the show with her Instagram followers, Sofia also posted a group photo, posing alongside CEO of Anastasia Beverly Hills Anastasia Soare, her daughter Norvina, and Sofia's niece Claudia Vergara.
