    Park Bo Ram’s death has been announced by her agency, after the star tragically passed away at just 30 years old.

    The singer who has been a beloved star in K-Pop for 10 years was found unconscious at a friend’s house. The celebrity was enjoying a private meet up with two other friends on the night she died, according to local reports. The music star was rushed to Hanyang University Guri Hospital after medics arrived, but was sadly pronounced dead.

    Park Bo Ram’s death confirmed by her agency

    According to a report filed by Namyangju Police Station today, (April 12), the alarm was raised when she didn’t return to the bathroom.

    She had gone to the restroom at around 9.55 PM local time but when she didn’t come out after some time, one of her friend’s went to check on her.

    They found her slouched over the sink and unconscious, they called emergency services and attended to perform CPR on the singer.

    The police have said they are investigating the cause of Park Bo Ram’s death and are requesting an autopsy with the National Forensic Service.

    Her agency has also confirmed the tragic news, and issued a statement.

    They said: “Park Bo Ram suddenly left us on the late evening of April 11. Our hearts are deeply troubled as we deliver this sudden news to her fans.

    “A wake and funeral proceedings will be scheduled after a discussion with the singer’s family.”

    Fans pay tribute to the young singer

    The news of Park Bo Ram’s death has hit fans hard, as they pay tribute to the K-Pop star on social media.

    One user on Twitter wrote: “Listening to Reply 1988 OST won’t be the same anymore. RIP Park Bo Ram”.

    A second wrote: “Oh my god, what happened?”

    A third said: “Rest in Peace Park Bo Ram. 1March 1994 – 12 April 2024. Your beautiful song will always be the most beautiful memory of life”.

    While a fourth echoed: “So sad, gone too soon, may her soul rest in peace”.
