    Hnk Gorica, originally known as NK Gorica, was founded in 1958 as a football club from Velika Gorica, Croatia. It started as a small local team and has since grown to become one of the prominent football clubs in the country.

    The Club’s Colors and Emblem

    The official colors of Hnk Gorica are green and white, which symbolize the club’s connection to the natural surroundings of the Gorica region. The club’s emblem features a football with crossed tennis rackets, representing the club’s origins as a merger between the football and tennis clubs in Velika Gorica.

    The home stadium of Hnk Gorica is the Gradski stadion Velika Gorica. The stadium has a seating capacity of over 5,000 spectators and provides an electric atmosphere during matches.
