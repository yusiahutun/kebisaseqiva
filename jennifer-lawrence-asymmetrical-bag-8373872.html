    In my book, Jennifer Lawrence is the queen of street style. She always steps out in a casual-chic outfit that’s uncomplicated yet totally put together. But the secret is in her accessories, whether she opts for a sleek black belt, sporty baseball cap, or luxe sunglasses. So, I wasn’t surprised to see her follow the same fashion equation a few days ago.

    Aupen 03. Fearless Re-Edition Bag

    Upon strolling through New York City, Lawrence wore a longline lapel coat, knit sweater vest over a white button-up shirt, and black trousers. She topped off the preppy ensemble with a black beanie and round sunglasses, but what really caught my attention was her $340 asymmetrical Fearless bag from Aupen. The funky purse featured sharp, geometric lines, rounded edges, and a short, thin strap. It captured both elegance and edginess, combining two very different aesthetics into one seamless accessory that elevated her otherwise standard outfit.  

    Aupen 01. Purpose Bag

    This isn’t the first time I’ve seen this modern bag style on display. Kylie Jenner and Gabrielle Union have carried the exact Aupen purse before, while Hailey Bieber and Dakota Johnson have styled a similar version from the brand, as well. In all instances, the geometric purse design offers an eye-catching take on the fundamental black bag, but is still easy to style. 

    Calvin Klein Basalt Flap Demi Shoulder Bag

    You don’t have to spend hundreds of dollars to achieve Lawrence’s look, though. Calvin Klein offers a spot-on lookalike, featuring a front-flap closure, adjustable strap, and metallic hardware. The leather-like exterior is classy and sophisticated and comes in six hues. Style the $95 bag with comfortable bottoms and a cozy trench coat, like Lawrence, or dress it up with platform combat boots and a sleek bodysuit. 

    Charles and Keith Asymmetrical Shoulder Bag

    The Jennifer Lopez-worn brand Charles and Keith makes a similar version, but in a fall-perfect hue. The maple-toned bag includes a glossy gold chain detail, smooth construction, and a zipper closure. This everyday purse would look great alongside a cream sweater, knee-high boots, and a warm cashmere scarf.   

    Travelon Signature Crossbody Purse 

    If you want the same trendy look but desire a more casual approach, consider Travelon’s Signature Crossbody Purse. The bag comes in four colors, includes two front-zip pockets, one large main compartment, and a comfortable carrying strap. This is a great pick for commuters and travelers since the spacious interior allows you to carry everything you need for a day out, while offering anti-theft features like slash-resistant material and lockable sections.  

    Lawrence — and tons of other celebs — just made a case for this spunky bag style, proving its versatility and ease. So, find the ideal asymmetrical bag to add to your collection.
