    Nicole Kidman, Lainey Wilson, Carly Pearce and many more looked incredible

      The biggest names in country music arrived in Nashville, Tennessee, on Wednesday for the 2023 CMA Awards – which meant there were some incredible looks on the red carpet.

      Held at the city's Bridgestone Arena, the likes of Lainey Wilson, Carly Pearce, Nicole Kidman, and so many more turned up to the star-studded event – dubbed 'country music's biggest night' – to witness unforgettable solo performances, never-before-seen collaborations, and special tributes.

      But before they sat down to enjoy the show, they served up some show-stopping fashion moments that certainly beat the red carpet looks from last year. While there were a few who opted for multi-colored ensembles, the most popular hues of the evening were black and red, and of course, there were plenty of sequins too. 

      Take a look at some of the most stylish red carpet arrivals below…

      2023 CMA Awards red carpet

      Lainey Wilson

      Lainey Wilson stole the show in her mind-blowing, strapless black jumpsuit that featured elaborate, tiered bell bottoms. In keeping with her signature style, she added a black cowboy hat and ornate silver jewelry. 

      Nicole Kidman

      Nicole Kidman turned up to support her husband, Keith Urban, and she looked incredible in a slinky black dress that exposed her toned stomach.

      Kelsea Ballerini

      Kelsea Ballerini looked like a Hollywood icon in her asymmetrical baby pink gown that highlighted her curves and long legs. Her pop of red lipstick added a touch of drama too. 

      Carly Pearce

      Carly Pearce rocked the color of the season in her metallic silver, sequinned skirt. The ' singer let the stunning creation be the focus of her outfit, teaming it with a black velvet bandeau top that featured a floor-sweeping train.

      Megan Moroney

      Double-nominee Megan Moroney had all eyes on her when she graced the red carpet in her billowing scarlet gown. The sensational dress screamed glamour but had a little edge with a thigh-high slit and plunging neckline.

      Cynthia Erivo

      Actress and singer, Cynthia Erivo, looked beautiful in her purple dress emblazoned with colorful motifs and an elegant train.

      Hillary Scott

      Lady A singer Hillary Scott also wore red for the evening and looked gorgeous in a silky midi wrap dress with matching heels.

      Bunnie Xo

      Jelly Roll's wife and podcaster, Bunnie Xo, looked phenomenal in her figure-hugging black dress that featured a sparkling embellishment, mesh sleeves, and a plunging neckline.

      Ashley McBryde

      The '' singer turned heads with her entrance in this floor-sweeping red gown that boasted a halter neck design to show off her various tattoos.

      Caroline Boyer

      CMA's host Luke Bryan's wife wowed in her metallic patterned dress that revealed her toned legs thanks to a daring thigh-high slit.

      Emily Ann Roberts

      season nine finalist and country music singer, Emily Ann Roberts looked stunning in her multi-colored, sequinned jumpsuit that boasted flared sleeves and cut-outs along her waist.

      Brittany Spencer

      The country singer oozed confidence in her sultry black velvet dress that boasted an off-the-shoulder design, center split, and ruched detailing at the waist.

      Hailey Whitters

      nominee Hailey Whitters channeled her inner 'Pretty Woman' in this incredible, floor-length red gown and white opera gloves. We love the elaborate ruffle detailing on the sleeves and neckline.

      Mickey Guyton

      The 'singer opted for a bold pop of color in her yellow mini dress that featured rhinestone embellishment, a cut-out under the bust, and a figure-hugging silhouette.
