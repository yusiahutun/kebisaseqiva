    When Dior's feminist T-shirt hit the catwalk during Maria Grazia Chiuri's debut for the brand last season, many fashion insiders predicted it would be an instant hit.

    And, as 2.6 million women marched around the world this weekend campaigning for gender equality in a Donald Trump era, it seems Dior really has tapped into a zeitgeist. So far, Rihanna, Natalie Portman and Jennifer Lawrence have worn the T-shirt, and we can expect plenty more to follow suit.

    The piece itself arrives in stores in mid-February; expect a lengthy waiting list.
