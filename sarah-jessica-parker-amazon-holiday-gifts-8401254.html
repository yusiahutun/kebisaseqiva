    Sarah Jessica Parker Told Me the Holiday Gifts She's Buying at Amazon This Season

    14 Mar 2024, 00:00

    It’s rare to meet a person — let alone a celebrity — who radiates warmth and light, but that’s exactly how I would describe my few moments spent with Sarah Jessica Parker. The actress and fashion designer attended Amazon’s Holiday Soirée last week and blessed us all with a storefront of her favorite holiday finds. While we just schmoozed in person, Parker gave me the inside scoop over email about the gifts she’s buying from Amazon this season — and prices start at just $10.  

    Parker is “big on giving thoughtful gifts,” she said, which is why she “starts thinking about holiday gifts super early.” This year, she’s been turning to Amazon’s Holiday Shop for inspiration, as “they have literally everything, something for everyone on your list.” Whether you’re shopping for your partner, friends, kids, or even yourself, you’ll find fool-proof gifts, below, with Parker’s expert stamp of approval.

    Sarah Jessica Parker-Loved Holiday Gifts

    • Koolaburra by Ugg Tizzey Slide Slipper, $70 (Originally $80)
    • Urpower Aroma Essential Oil Diffuser, $28 with coupon (Originally $36)
    • DoTerra Patchouli Essential Oil, $43
    • RoC Multi Correxion Revive and Glow 10 Percent Active Vitamin C Serum, $19 (Originally $35)
    • Baimei Gua Sha and Jade Roller Set, $10 (Originally $16)
    • Beats Studio Pro Wireless Bluetooth Noise-Canceling Headphones, $300 (Originally $350)
    • JBL Charge 4 Waterproof Portable Bluetooth Speaker, $105 (Originally $150)
    • SJP by Sarah Jessica Parker Celine Block Heel Mary Jane Pump, $194 (Originally $465)
    • Le Creuset 2.75-Quart Enameled Cast Iron Shallow Round Oven, $290
    • Serge Normant Dream Big Hairspray, $25
    • Dinil Spell and Chime Candles 40-Pack, $12

    Koolaburra by Ugg Tizzey Slide Slipper

    A “good slipper” makes a great gift, according to Parker, and she’s “really loving” the Koolaburra by Ugg Tizzey Slide Slippers. They’re made from suede with a faux fur lining, and they have rubber soles, so you can wear them both inside and out. A shopper even confirmed the slippers are the “softest ever,” adding that they’re “extremely comfortable,” and the “quality is amazing.” As Parker says: “Ugg never fails.” 

    Urpower Aroma Essential Oil Diffuser

    When asked what she’s buying for herself this holiday season, Parker said she can “never have too many oil diffusers and essential oils.” She’s especially a fan of the Urpower Aroma Essential Oil Diffuser, which is on sale for $28 with an on-site coupon. It has room for 300 milliliters of oil, can display seven LED light colors, and has four misting modes with an automatic timer. Pair the electric diffuser with Parker's go-to DoTerra essential oil, which she calls a “real treat.” 

    RoC Multi Correxion Revive and Glow 10 Percent Active Vitamin C Serum

    Parker said she’s “picky about [her] skincare products,” but the RoC Multi Correxion vitamin C serum has “quickly become a favorite.” Not only is it formulated with brightening vitamin C, but it also has peptides to tighten and smooth out your skin. For even more firming benefits, Parker pairs the serum with an ice-cold jade roller — the “perfect combination, morning or evening,” she said. Wrap up the serum and roller together as a gift for the beauty lover in your life.

    Beats Studio Pro Wireless Bluetooth Noise-Canceling Headphones

    For her college-aged son, Parker “can always find something” in Amazon’s tech department, and this holiday season, she’s eyeing the Beats Studio Pro Wireless Bluetooth Noise-Canceling Headphones. They have up to 40 hours of battery life, per the brand, and the “sound quality is fantastic,” according to a reviewer. This JBL bluetooth speaker is another “great” tech gift, Parker said, especially for someone who’s frequently “on the go.” 

    SJP by Sarah Jessica Parker Celine Block Heel Mary Jane Pump

    The SJP by Sarah Jessica Parker Celine Mary Jane Pump is a “particularly festive shoe,” according to the designer herself, and it just so happens to be on sale for 58 percent off at Amazon. The sparkly shoes have a 2.75-inch block heel, a rounded toe, and an adjustable strap across the top with a jeweled statement buckle. A reviewer described the heels as “beautiful shoes that are also comfortable,” which is exactly what you want if you plan to wear them for a holiday party. These Mary Jane pumps might just be the perfect outfit-completing gift to yourself this season.
