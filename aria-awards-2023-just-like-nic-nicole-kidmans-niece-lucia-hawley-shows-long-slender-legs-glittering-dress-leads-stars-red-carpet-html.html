    01 Jun 2024, 00:00

    Australia's music stars descended on Sydney's Hordern Pavilion on Wednesday for the 2023 ARIA Awards.

    And Nicole Kidman's niece Lucia Hawley turned heads as she arrived early for the show. 

    The 25-year-old flaunted her trim pins in a sparkly red frock as she posed on the red carpet.

    Lucia's mini dress featured two floral appliques and cut-out detailing along the bust.

    She accessorised her look with a pair of white heels and statement earrings.

    ARIA Awards 2023: Nicole Kidman's niece Lucia Hawley showed off her long slender legs in a glittering dress as she lead the stars on the red carpet

    Lucia is the daughter of Antonia Kidman, 53, a former television presenter-turned-lawyer who is the sister of Oscar-winning actress Nicole Kidman. 

    Her grandfather was Antony Kidman, the late Australian psychologist and academic, who died from a heart attack in 2014 at age 75.

    Lucia recently explained why she shouldn't be labelled a 'nepo baby' despite her enviable showbiz connections.

    As the new face of Live From E!, the aspiring journalist attends the biggest events in Australia, interviewing celebrities and reporting on the hottest fashion moments.

    Lucia's mini dress featured two floral appliques and cut-out detailing along the bust 

    While some may accuse Lucia of using her 'nepo baby' connections to further her media career, she insists she worked hard to land her 'dream job'.

    'I've been working towards this,' she told The Sunday Telegraph.

    The term 'nepo baby', short for 'nepotism baby', stems from a recent article published by New York magazine calling out U.S. celebrities who used their famous parents and relatives to build their careers.

    Lucia has followed in her mother Antonia's footsteps, who worked as a journalist for Channel 9 and NBN Television.

    She accessorised her look with a pair of white heels and statement earrings 

    Speaking to Woman's Day last April, the young journalist said her family connections have made her more determined to prove herself in the industry.

    'I would never deny my privilege and I actually, totally hold my hand up and say, ''yeah I'm privileged, I come from a really privileged family and I'm so grateful for the opportunities that have come my way because of it,"' she said.

    'The only thing I can do is work hard and make an individual name for myself and prove myself.'

    Discussing her 'nepo baby' title again, she added: 'If that's the worst thing being said about me, then so be it.'
