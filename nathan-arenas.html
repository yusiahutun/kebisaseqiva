    Nathan Arenas is an actor from the United States. Moreover, Nathan Arenas is famous for portraying the role of Jorge in the Disney Channel original series, ‘BUNK’D.’

    Early Life

    Nathan Arenas was born on the 30th of September 2005, in Los Angeles, California, the United States of America. According to his birth date, he is 17 years old and holds American nationality. Likewise, he belongs to a Multiracial ethnicity and has a birth sign of Libra.

    He is the son of Alejandra Arenas (mother) and his father’s name is unknown at the moment.  Currently, he lives with his mother and a young sister named Allyson Juliette in California. His younger sister is also a child artist.

    Professional Career

    Nathan Arenas has had a childhood interest in acting. He began learning acting at his acting school from his mentor, actor, and director David DeLuise. In 2015, he appeared in his first audition for the Disney Channel’s sitcom “BUNK’D,” where he was cast as Jorge.

    That comedy show has been going effectively for two seasons. Because he is a funny actor, he was nominated for the Best Television Young Actor Award for his work as Jorge in “BUNK’D.” He has also worked as a criminal mind in crime thrillers.

    Aside from that, he has appeared on an episode of ‘Hollywood Today Live’ and in additional Disney merchandise campaigns. He has been on various television shows such as ‘New Girl’ and ‘Disney Monstober.’
