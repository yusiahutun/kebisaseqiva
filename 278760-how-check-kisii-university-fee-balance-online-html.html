    The process of inquiring your fee balance at Kisii University

    Most people, especially old school folks, know what a university fee deadline means. On this basis, I am determined to provide Kisii University comrades with a summarized procedure on how to access their fee balance with ease. Firstly, make sure you register your email with the varsity to become eligible for a student’s account then follow the lead below to access your financial details.

    • Sign up for a student account with Kisii University through the varsity portal. If you have problems at this stage, liaise with the ICT department to help you obtain an account securely.
    • Sign in to your university student account using your student identification details
    • Open a secure browser on your phone, tablet, or personal computer
    • Navigate the URL section in your browser then enter www.kisiiuniversity.ac.ke
    • On the homepage, navigate and open “fee balance online”. The link will then re-direct you to www.taaco.org/kuc/login.php for further authentication and login.
    • Log in using your student registration number and student identification number. Provide the password as kuc/KUC then submit your login details.

    Messaging system for checking fee balance

    Kisii University college fee balance inquiry protocol has evolved rapidly to become one of the most effective in the region. Kisii University fee balance inquiry is now possible using the messaging number systems. Students are allowed to send a short message to the message number inquiring about the fee status. The system was first introduced in 2015 by Francis Monari, the dean of student at the university.

    The vital thing about the system is that the system accepts messages from all the mobile networks in the country and gives immediate feedback with minimal delays. The decision to introduce the system arrived at considering the rising enrollment of students in the university.

    The massaging system in the university meant to save on lecture time by solving the problem of long queues at the finance offices. Students are instructed to send the messages incorrect formats for them to get the feedback as expected. Using any mobile operator, send the message in the form; “ksu admission number” to message number 20750, (ksu ADM NO to 20750). The system allows students to check their fee balance even when they are not in session.
